# SEO Longtail 20 KPI Checklist

Use this checklist for the first 14 days after publishing the initial 20 longtail guides.

## 1) Indexing Health (Day 3, 7, 14)

- [ ] Submitted sitemap and requested indexing for 5 priority URLs.
- [ ] Indexed URL count for this batch is at least 70%.
- [ ] No unexpected `noindex`, canonical mismatch, or blocked crawling.
- [ ] 404 and redirect errors are not increasing materially.

## 2) Search Performance (Day 7, 14)

- [ ] Track impressions/clicks/CTR for the 20 URL set.
- [ ] Batch-level CTR target: 2.5%+ (initial), 4%+ (improved metadata).
- [ ] At least 30% of pages earn non-zero impressions by Day 7.
- [ ] At least 10 pages rank within top 50 for at least one query by Day 14.

## 3) On-Page Engagement

- [ ] Internal click-through from longtail guide to school/university pages is rising.
- [ ] Bounce-sensitive pages have stronger intro and clearer next action.
- [ ] Top underperforming 5 pages receive title/meta/intro refresh.

## 4) Iteration Decision

- [ ] If indexed >= 70% and CTR >= 2.5%, expand to +40 pages (total 60).
- [ ] If indexed < 70%, fix sitemap/internal links before volume expansion.
- [ ] If CTR < 2.5%, improve title/description templates and re-test.

## Suggested Reporting View

- URL
- Indexed (Y/N)
- Impressions
- Clicks
- CTR
- Average Position
- Internal Clicks to `/school/*` or `/universities`
- Action (Keep / Improve / Consolidate)
