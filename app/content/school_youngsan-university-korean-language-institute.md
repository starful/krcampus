## School Overview
Youngsan University (YSU) Korean Language Institute offers high-quality Korean language education tailored for international students wishing to study in South Korea. Located in the beautiful coastal city of Busan (specifically at the Haeundae Campus, which specializes in tourism, culinary arts, and cultural content), the institute provides an immersive environment where students can master the Korean language while enjoying the vibrant lifestyle of Korea's second-largest metropolis.

## Curriculum and Levels
The Regular Program consists of 4 semesters per year (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter), each spanning 10 weeks (200 hours of instruction). The curriculum is systematically structured into 6 levels (Level 1 to Level 6) conforming to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) and TOPIK guidelines:
- **Beginner (Levels 1-2):** Essential survival Korean, basic grammar, and daily conversational skills.
- **Intermediate (Levels 3-4):** Communication in public spaces, understanding of Korean society, and intensive preparation for academic life.
- **Advanced (Levels 5-6):** Professional-grade Korean, academic research, and reading of news/literature.

## Special Features & Student Support
- **TOPIK Centered Classes:** Regular mock tests and supplementary lectures to help students achieve target TOPIK scores for university entry.
- **Excellent Dormitory Access:** International students are prioritized for comfortable on-campus housing equipped with modern amenities.
- **Cultural Programs:** Every semester includes field trips, traditional Korean craft making, K-Pop dance classes, and historical tours around Busan and Gyeongju.
- **Pathway to Degree Programs:** Graduates of the Korean Language Institute receive exclusive tuition discount scholarships and streamlined admission when advancing to Youngsan University’s undergraduate or graduate schools.
