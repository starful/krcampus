## University Overview

Established in 1947, **Kangwon National University (KNU)** is one of South Korea's ten flagship national universities. Operating across three campuses in Gangwon Province—with the main campus located in the vibrant lakeside city of Chuncheon, and regional campuses in Samcheok and Dogye—KNU represents academic excellence, affordable education, and a deeply supportive community for global talents.

KNU is highly recognized for its extensive research output, green-campus initiative, and strong academic-industrial collaborations. It offers a balanced mixture of traditional academic rigor and futuristic tech integration, making it a prime destination for international students looking to study in South Korea without the high living costs of Seoul.

## Why International Students Choose KNU

### 1. Exceptional Quality at an Affordable Price
As a flagship national university, KNU benefits from heavy government funding. This allows the university to maintain incredibly low tuition fees (roughly half the cost of private universities in Seoul) while maintaining state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, libraries, and campus amenities.

### 2. Proximity to Seoul with a Peaceful Environment
Located in Chuncheon, the capital of Gangwon Province, KNU offers the perfect blend of scenic nature and modern urban living. Chuncheon is famous for its beautiful lakes, mountains, and parks. Thanks to the ITX-Cheongchun express train, students can reach metropolitan Seoul in under an hour, giving them easy access to the capital's culture while enjoying a cleaner, safer, and cheaper residential environment.

### 3. Comprehensive Global Support
KNU's Office of International Affairs provides tailored services to help international students adjust to life in Korea. Programs include:
- **KNU Buddy Program:** Pairing incoming international students with local Korean students for cultural exchange and academic transition support.
- **Free Korean Language Support:** Extra tutoring and preparation classes for the TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean).
- **International Student Dormitory Guarantee:** Priority housing for incoming freshman and transfer students.

## Key Academic Fields & Strengths

KNU boasts strong academic programs across diverse fields, but is uniquely distinguished in:
- **Forest and Environmental Sciences:** Utilizing Gangwon Province's rich natural landscapes, KNU runs top-tier research institutes focused on ecology, climate change, and timber engineering.
- **Agriculture and Life Sciences:** Pioneering biotechnology and smart-farming research.
- **Information Technology & Engineering:** Close collaborations with major industrial clusters in Gangwon and the Seoul capital area.
- **Korean Language and Culture Programs:** Highly rated intensive language courses that help prepare students for university-level academic coursework.

## Admissions and Scholarships for International Students

KNU runs a holistic admissions process for international applicants twice a year (Spring and Fall semesters). 

### Language Requirements
- **Korean Track:** Minimum TOPIK Level 3 (TOPIK Level 4 is recommended for graduation, particularly in humanities and business fields).
- **English Track:** For specific programs taught in English, minimum scores of TOEFL iBT 80, IELTS 5.5, or CEFR B2 are required.

### Scholarship Opportunities
KNU offers a wide variety of scholarships to reduce the financial burden on global students:
- **Best KNU Global Scholarship:** Awarded to top-tier applicants, covering 100% of tuition and fees.
- **Academic Excellence Scholarships:** Tuition waivers ranging from 20% to 80% based on GPA performance in previous semesters.
- **TOPIK Achievement Scholarships:** Cash incentives or tuition discounts offered to students who successfully raise their TOPIK scores during their studies.
