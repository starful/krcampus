## University Overview

Kookmin University (KMU) is the first private university established after South Korea's independence from Japan, founded in 1946 by cabinet members of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Korea. Located in Seongbuk-gu, Seoul, KMU has grown into a prestigious institution hosting over 23,000 students, including a vibrant community of more than 2,700 international students.

KMU is highly recognized for its student-centered educational philosophy, 'Pragmatism', which bridges academic knowledge with industry demands.

## Why Choose Kookmin University?

- **Global Leader in Design and Automotive Engineering**: Kookmin is widely regarded as one of the best universities in Korea for Industrial Design, Graphic Design, and Automotive Engineering. The university maintains deep collaborations with global brands like Hyundai, Kia, and Samsung.
- **Scenic and Accessible Campus**: The campus directly borders Bukhansan National Park, offering one of the most beautiful, green, and serene academic environments in Seoul while remaining easily accessible to the downtown area.
- **Substantial Support for International Students**: From dedicated English-track programs to Korean language support and tailored job fairs, Kookmin provides a welcoming ecosystem for global talent.

## Popular Programs for International Students

1. **College of Design**: Famed nationwide for producing top-tier designers. Majors include Industrial Design, Visual Communication Design, and Metalwork & Jewelry.
2. **College of Automotive Engineering**: Highly specialized, with massive funding from the government and corporate partners.
3. **KMU International Business School (KIBS)**: Fully taught in English, offering global perspectives on trade, business, and economics.
4. **College of Software**: A government-designated 'Software Centered University' program that equips students with modern coding, AI, and big data skills.

## Campus Life & Facilities

Kookmin University offers modern dormitories (both on-campus and off-campus), comprehensive library systems, and excellent dining options. The student union operates various international student clubs, counseling centers, and peer-mentoring programs to help newcomers assimilate smoothly into Korean culture.

## Admissions & Scholarship Opportunities

International applicants can apply for Fall and Spring semesters. Admission criteria typically rely on language proficiency (TOPIK Level 3+ or English certificates like IELTS/TOEFL for English tracks) and academic records.

- **Incoming Scholarships**: Up to 50% to 100% of tuition waived for students with high TOPIK scores (Level 4 or above) or outstanding academic records.
- **Academic Excellence Scholarships**: Awarded every semester based on GPA, helping students minimize financial burdens throughout their studies.
