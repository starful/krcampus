## School Overview

The University of Seoul (UOS) International Language Institute (ILI) is a premier public educational institution located in Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul. Managed and funded by the Seoul Metropolitan Government, the university is highly regarded for providing premium-quality education at a remarkably affordable cost compared to private institutions in Seoul.

Established to foster global communication, the International Language Institute offers systematically structured Korean language programs taught by highly experienced, certified faculty members. UOS is known for its beautiful, green, and spacious campus, which offers a peaceful sanctuary within the bustling city of Seoul.

## Curriculum & Academic Programs

### Regular Korean Language Program
The flagship program runs on a 4-term system (Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter). It is designed to develop comprehensive communication skills across six levels (Level 1 to Level 6).
- **Schedule:** 10 weeks per term, 200 total hours (Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM).
- **Key Focus Areas:** Speaking, Listening, Reading, Writing, and Grammar integrated with real-life practical conversation.
- **Support:** Supplementary TOPIK preparation classes are offered to help students transition into undergraduate or graduate programs.

### Short-Term Program
A specialized intensive course spanning 3 weeks during the summer break, specifically focused on rapid conversational development and immersive Korean cultural exploration.

## Cultural Experience & Student Support

- **Cultural Activities:** Every term, the institute organizes unique cultural field trips, including visits to historical landmarks, Korean cooking classes, traditional pottery making, K-Pop dance workshops, and traditional craft making.
- **UOS Peer Buddy Program:** International students are matched with local UOS undergraduate students. This initiative helps students practice daily conversational Korean, make local friends, and quickly adapt to life in Korea.
- **Facilities:** Students enrolled in the language program have full access to university amenities, including state-of-the-art libraries, computer labs, student cafeterias, and gym facilities.

## Accommodation

UOS offers comfortable on-campus housing options (Residence Hall and International House) for language students. While rooms are assigned based on availability, prioritizing international students allows many to enjoy safe, convenient, and highly affordable living spaces just steps away from their classrooms.
