# Guide to Chungnam National University Korean Language Center

## 1. School Overview
Chungnam National University (CNU) is a premier flagship national university in South Korea. Located in Daejeon, the nation’s science and technology hub, the CNU Korean Language Center operates inside the modern Language Education Center (W12) building. With an official capacity of 1,000 international students, the center delivers high-quality linguistic instruction combined with rich cultural exposure.

Choosing CNU offers several strategic advantages:
* **Flagship National University:** High academic reputation across Korea.
* **Affordable Tuition & Living Costs:** Lower overall expenses compared to Seoul-based universities.
* **On-Campus Dormitory Access:** Priority housing provided to language trainees.
* **Daejeon Science & Tech Hub Location:** Safe, research-focused city with excellent public transit.
* **Structured TOPIK Preparation:** Targeted test prep to guarantee academic success.

**Address:** Language Education Center (W12), Chungnam National University, 99 Daehak-ro, Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, 34134, Republic of Korea.

## 4. Admissions & D-4 Visa Steps
Students enrolling in the Regular Program for two or more terms must apply for a D-4 (General Student) visa.

### Step-by-Step Pathway
1. **Document Submission:** Submit the application form, copy of passport, graduation certificate (apostilled/consular-verified), and a bank balance certificate of USD 10,000+.
2. **Screening & Interview:** The admissions committee reviews the academic and financial documents.
3. **Tuition Payment:** Upon receiving an invoice, wire-transfer the tuition (minimum of two terms: 2,600,000 KRW) plus the application fee.
4. **Standard Admission Letter:** Receive your Certificate of Admission (CoA) from CNU via mail.
5. **D-4 Visa Application:** Present the CoA, passport, and financial documents to the nearest South Korean Embassy to obtain your visa.

## 5. TOPIK & University Pathway
CNU runs a highly structured TOPIK pathway to help students qualify for degree programs.
* **TOPIK Special Prep:** The course starting in October focuses on TOPIK II essay writing and listening practice.
* **Undergraduate Matriculation:** Students who reach TOPIK Level 3 or 4 can transition directly into undergraduate majors at CNU.
* **Tuition Scholarships:** Incoming students with high TOPIK scores (Level 4+) are eligible for partial to full tuition waivers during their first semester of university studies.

---

## 6. Dormitory & Living in the City
Daejeon offers high living standards at a more accessible price point than Seoul.
* **On-Campus Dormitory Access:** Language students are eligible to apply for on-campus dormitory housing. Rooms are shared (double occupancy) and include desks, beds, climate control, and campus internet access.
* **Daejeon Science & Tech Hub:** Located in Yuseong-gu, the campus is vibrant and surrounded by student-friendly infrastructure, hot springs, and parks.
* **Transportation:** Yuseong Spa Subway Station links the campus directly to Daejeon Station (KTX), allowing you to travel to Seoul in 50 minutes.

## 7. FAQ

### Can I work part-time while studying at CNU?
Yes. After 6 months of study on a D-4 visa, you can apply for a legal part-time work permit through the immigration office if your attendance is above 90%.

### Is the dormitory guaranteed for all language students?
While CNU prioritizes new international arrivals for on-campus housing, placements are limited. Students should apply immediately after paying their tuition.

### Can I get a refund if my D-4 visa is rejected?
Yes. If your visa is officially denied, submit a refund request along with the rejection slip. CNU will refund the tuition, though the registration fee is non-refundable.

### Are textbooks included in the course fees?
No. Textbooks are not included in the program tuition and must be purchased at the university bookstore for approximately 50,000 KRW per level.

### How do I travel to CNU from Incheon International Airport?
The easiest way is to take an intercity airport bus directly from Incheon Airport to the Daejeon Yuseong Bus Terminal, which is located close to the CNU campus.
