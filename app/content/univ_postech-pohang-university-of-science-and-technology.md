## 1. University Overview
Established in 1986 by POSCO, one of the world's leading steelmakers, Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH) has rapidly ascended to become a global powerhouse in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Located at 77 Cheongam-ro, Nam-gu, Pohang-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do, Republic of Korea, POSTECH was founded with a singular, ambitious mission: to provide an elite, research-focused education that rivals top-tier Western institutions.

Unlike massive public universities, POSTECH maintains an intentionally small, highly exclusive student body. With an acceptance rate of just 5% to 10%, it is one of the most selective universities in Asia. This small-scale approach enables an extraordinary faculty-to-student ratio of approximately 1:4, ensuring that undergraduate and graduate students receive unprecedented personalized mentorship, direct access to cutting-edge laboratories, and close academic support from world-renowned faculty members.

## 2. English-Taught & International Programs
As a trailblazer in Korean higher education, POSTECH established its official Bilingual Campus policy to foster a truly globalized academic environment. Almost all major-level undergraduate courses and nearly 100% of graduate-level courses are conducted entirely in English. This policy removes the language barrier for international students, allowing seamless academic integration from day one.

To support its international community (currently numbering around 250 elite students from various nations), the International Student and Scholar Services (ISSS) provides comprehensive transition assistance. This includes visa guidance, airport pickups, mandatory orientation, and complimentary Korean language and culture courses designed to help international scholars quickly adapt to campus life and navigate daily routines in Korea.

## 3. Faculties & Academic Strengths
POSTECH's academic structure is built specifically for deep scientific inquiry and disruptive technological innovation. The university hosts some of the world's most advanced research infrastructure, including the Pohang Light Source (PLS-II), a massive third-generation synchrotron radiation facility that attracts top-tier researchers worldwide.

## 5. Admissions for International Students
Securing admission to POSTECH is highly competitive, reflecting its 5% to 10% acceptance rate. The university looks for students who demonstrate not only academic brilliance but also a profound passion for research and innovation.

### Application Requirements
1. **Academic Records**: High school transcripts (for undergraduate) or university transcripts and degrees (for graduate) with exceptional GPAs.
2. **English Proficiency**: Standardized tests such as TOEFL iBT (minimum 79) or IELTS (minimum 6.0). Exemptions apply to native English speakers.
3. **Letters of Recommendation**: Letters from academic advisors or science/math teachers highlighting research capability.
4. **Personal Statement & Study Plan**: A detailed essay outlining research interests, academic goals, and why POSTECH is the ideal fit.
5. **Interview**: Shortlisted candidates must undergo an online academic interview conducted by department faculty.

## 6. Campus Life & Location
Located in Pohang, a scenic coastal city in Gyeongsangbuk-do, POSTECH offers a serene environment ideal for focused academic pursuits. While Pohang is a major global industrial city (home to POSCO's main steelworks), the university campus itself is a peaceful, self-contained sanctuary. 

All international students are guaranteed on-campus housing in the university dormitories. The Jigok Community Center functions as the social hub of the campus, featuring diverse dining options, student lounges, and convenience stores. With over 60 active student-run clubs—ranging from music and traditional Korean martial arts to robotics and startup incubators—international students can easily integrate into campus life, balance their rigorous academic workloads, and form lifelong connections.

## 7. FAQ

### Do I need to speak Korean to study at POSTECH?
No. POSTECH operates a Bilingual Campus where nearly all major undergraduate courses and all graduate courses are taught in English. However, learning basic Korean is highly recommended for daily life off-campus.

### Is housing guaranteed for international students?
Yes, POSTECH guarantees on-campus dormitory accommodation for all newly admitted international students, offering safe, affordable, and fully furnished living spaces.

### How do graduate students secure research funding?
Most international graduate students are admitted under the Research Assistantship (RA) or Teaching Assistantship (TA) framework. This covers 100% of their tuition fees and provides a regular monthly living stipend.

### What is the exact acceptance rate for international applicants?
POSTECH is highly selective, maintaining an overall acceptance rate of 5% to 10%. Selection criteria heavily emphasize analytical skills, academic transcripts, and a solid research foundation.

### Are there part-time job opportunities on campus?
Yes, international students holding a D-2 visa can work part-time in campus libraries, administrative offices, or language centers, subject to immigration regulations and university credit requirements.
