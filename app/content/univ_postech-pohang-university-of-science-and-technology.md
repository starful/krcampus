# Ultimate International Student Guide: Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH)

Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), founded in 1986 by steelmaker POSCO in Pohang, South Korea, is a premier global research institution. Despite its short history, POSTECH has quickly ascended to become a top-tier STEM university. With selective enrollment, an elite faculty-to-student ratio, and immense corporate backing, it offers international students a world-class, research-intensive environment.

---

## 1. University Overview

POSTECH was established to educate elite science and engineering minds and pioneer breakthrough technologies. Unlike massive comprehensive universities, POSTECH maintains an intimate, specialized environment with approximately 1,400 undergraduate and 2,100 graduate students. 

This boutique approach enables an exceptional 1:5 faculty-to-student ratio. Students receive direct mentorship from world-class researchers and can join cutting-edge lab research starting their first semester. The campus hosts around 280 international students. Additionally, POSTECH's close partnership with POSCO drives robust industry-academic research, providing students with extensive funding, internships, and direct career pathways in tech and materials science.

---

## 2. English-Taught & International Programs

As South Korea's first bilingual campus, POSTECH conducts nearly all junior/senior undergraduate courses and 100% of graduate courses in English. International students do not need prior Korean language skills to succeed academically.

The International Student and Scholar Services (ISSS) office assists with visas, cultural integration, and academic advising. To aid transition, POSTECH also provides free Korean language and culture courses alongside core STEM classes.

---

## 3. Faculties & Academic Strengths

POSTECH focuses entirely on advanced science and engineering, encouraging interdisciplinary research across 12 departments:

*   **Basic Sciences:** Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Life Sciences
*   **Core Engineering:** Materials Science, Mechanical, Industrial & Management, Electrical, Computer Science, Chemical Engineering
*   **Specialized & Convergence:** Convergence IT, Semiconductor Engineering

### Key Departmental Highlights

| Department | Key Focus Areas | Unique Research Facilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Semiconductor Engineering** | Microchip design, next-gen memory, fabrication | Cleanrooms, Nanotechnology Research Center |
| **Materials Science & Engineering** | Metallurgy, nanomaterials, biomaterials | Pohang Light Source (PLS-II) Synchrotron |
| **Computer Science & Engineering** | Artificial Intelligence, big data, cybersecurity | POSTECH AI Research Institute |
| **Convergence IT Engineering** | IoT, smart healthcare, human-computer interaction | Creative IT Design Studio |

POSTECH is home to the **Pohang Accelerator Laboratory (PAL)**, housing 3rd and 4th generation synchrotron radiation accelerators, giving students direct access to world-class light source facilities.

### Scholarship Opportunities
Most international students receive financial aid:
*   **POSTECH International Scholarship:** Covers 100% of tuition and provides a monthly living stipend for top-tier applicants.
*   **Global Korea Scholarship (GKS):** Government scholarship covering full tuition, airfare, and living allowances.
*   **Research & Teaching Assistantships (RA/TA):** Widely available for graduate students to cover living costs.

## 5. Admissions for International Students

Admissions are highly selective, with an international acceptance rate of **15-20%**. 

### Application Requirements
1.  **Academic Transcripts:** High GPA, specifically in mathematics and science.
2.  **Standardized Tests (Optional):** SAT, AP, ACT, or IB scores.
3.  **English Proficiency:** TOEFL iBT (min 79), IELTS (min 6.0), or equivalent (waived for native speakers or English-medium high schools).
4.  **Letters of Recommendation:** Two letters from science/math teachers.
5.  **Personal Statement & Study Plan:** Detailing research ambitions and alignment with POSTECH.

### Admissions Timeline
*   **Spring Intake (Starts March):** Applications open August to September of the previous year.
*   **Fall Intake (Starts September):** Applications open February to March of the same year.

## 6. Campus Life & Location

Located at **77 Cheongam-ro, Nam-gu, Pohang-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do**, POSTECH is situated in a prominent coastal industrial city. Seoul is 2.5 hours away via KTX.

### Key Campus Features
*   **Residential College (RC):** Guaranteed, subsidized housing for all undergraduates, integrating academic support and community living.
*   **Tae-Joon Park Digital Library:** A state-of-the-art, 6-story library open 24/7 with collaborative workspaces.
*   **Sports Facilities:** Includes an indoor pool, golf ranges, squash courts, and soccer fields.
*   **POSCO Partnership Hubs:** On-campus startup incubators for commercializing academic projects.

## 7. FAQ

### Can I apply to POSTECH if I don't speak any Korean?
Yes. POSTECH is bilingual. Almost all upper-level undergraduate and all graduate courses, as well as administrative services, are in English.

### What is the acceptance rate for international students?
The international acceptance rate is 15-20%. Selection is highly competitive and focuses on academic performance in science and math.

### Are international students guaranteed housing on campus?
Yes, POSTECH guarantees heavily subsidized on-campus dormitory housing for all admitted international students.

### Can I work part-time while studying at POSTECH?
Yes. Under a D-2 visa, you can work up to 20 hours per week during semesters with university and immigration approval. On-campus roles include lab assistants and tutoring.

### What are the career prospects for international graduates in Korea?
Excellent. Thanks to deep ties with POSCO, Samsung, and Hyundai, graduates easily transition to research or engineering roles via D-10 or E-7 visas, or pursue PhDs globally.
