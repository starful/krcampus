## University Overview
Established in 1946, **Kyungpook National University (KNU)** is one of the premier Flagship East Asian Research Universities and a highly respected national institution in South Korea. Located primarily in Daegu, the third-largest metropolitan area in the country, KNU has earned a stellar reputation for academic excellence, pioneering research, and producing global leaders.

KNU is widely recognized for its high-caliber faculty, extensive research funding, and its vast campus that provides an ideal environment for academic pursuits. For international students, KNU offers a balanced mix of traditional Korean academic heritage and a globalized curriculum, all at a fraction of the cost of private universities in Seoul.

---

## Why Choose Kyungpook National University?

### 1. Affordable, High-Quality Education
As a national university heavily subsidized by the South Korean government, KNU offers exceptionally low tuition rates while maintaining world-class educational standards. Undergraduate tuition averages around **KRW 4,000,000 to 5,500,000 per year**, which is about half the cost of major private universities in Seoul. 

### 2. Global IT and Engineering Hub
KNU is located in close proximity to major industrial zones in the Daegu-Gyeongbuk area, home to global conglomerates like Samsung, LG, and POSCO. The university's **School of IT Engineering** and semiconductor-related majors are particularly famous, enjoying strong corporate partnerships that translate into high employment rates, internship opportunities, and modern laboratory environments for engineering students.

### 3. Generous International Scholarships
KNU is highly welcoming to global talent. Through the **KNU Honors Scholarship** program, incoming international students are awarded 50% to 100% tuition waivers based on their language proficiency test scores (TOPIK, TOEFL, IELTS). There are also academic performance scholarships available for current students every semester.

### 4. Vibrant Campus & Location
The main Daegu campus is a beautiful, green city-within-a-city. Daegu is known for its rich history, distinct local culture, warm weather, and convenient transportation network. It offers a much lower cost of living than Seoul while still providing all the luxuries, transit connections, and nightlife of a major metropolis.

## Core Academics and Popular Majors

KNU comprises 17 colleges and 14 graduate schools, spread across its main Daegu campus and the specialized Sangju campus.

* **College of IT Engineering:** Renowned nationwide for computer science, electronic engineering, and mobile systems technology.
* **School of Business Administration:** AACSB-accredited, ensuring a world-class standard in business, management, and finance programs.
* **College of Agriculture and Life Sciences:** One of Korea's oldest agricultural colleges, leading research in biotechnology and smart farming.
* **Korean Language and Literature:** A top destination for students looking to specialize in Korean studies and education.

---

## Student Life and Housing

### Accommodation
KNU offers comfortable and safe on-campus housing options. The modern **Cheomseong-gwan** and **Munhwa-gwan** dormitories are highly sought after by international students. These dorms feature double-occupancy rooms with private bathrooms, high-speed internet, fitness centers, study halls, and dining halls serving diverse menu options.

### Support Services
* **International Writing Center:** Helps foreign students polish their academic papers, essays, and reports in both English and Korean.
* **Buddy Program:** Pairs newly arrived international students with domestic KNU students to ease their transition into Korean university life and culture.
* **Global Lounge:** A dedicated cultural exchange space on campus designed for international events, language exchange, and relaxation.

---

## Admission Guidelines for International Students

### Language Requirements
* **Korean Track:** TOPIK Level 3 or higher is generally required for admission, though Level 4+ is recommended for comfortable academic progress.
* **English Track:** For programs taught entirely in English, applicants must provide valid English proficiency test scores (typically TOEFL iBT 80, IELTS 5.5, or CEFR B2 equivalent).

### Application Timeline
* **Spring Semester (Starts in March):** Applications open around September to October of the preceding year.
* **Fall Semester (Starts in September):** Applications open around March to April of the same year.
