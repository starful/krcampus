# Konkuk University: Ultimate International Student Guide

Located in the heart of Seoul, Konkuk University (KU) is one of South Korea's premier private institutions. Known for its blend of academic rigor, massive green campus, and unmatched location, Konkuk has become a top choice for global talents. Today, the university hosts over 3,200 international students, offering a supportive ecosystem designed to turn global perspectives into academic and professional success.

---

## 1. University Overview

Established in 1946, Konkuk University has evolved from a school dedicated to rural development into a comprehensive global research hub. Situated at **120 Neungdong-ro, Gwangjin-gu, Seoul**, Konkuk balances urban energy with campus serenity. 

The campus is famous for **Ilkamho**, a massive central lake that serves as the university's scenic landmark. Unlike many hilly Seoul campuses, Konkuk is remarkably flat, making it exceptionally pedestrian-friendly.

## 2. English-Taught & International Programs

While the majority of degree programs at Konkuk are taught in Korean, the university has rapidly expanded its English-language offerings. 

* **English-Track Majors:** Selected departments within the College of Business Administration and the College of Engineering offer courses in English. 
* **Korean Language Institute (KLI):** For students who do not meet the Korean proficiency requirements, Konkuk’s KLI offers intensive language programs. Completing Level 3 or higher at KLI unlocks direct entry pathways into undergraduate majors.
* **Exchange & Study Abroad:** Konkuk partners with over 500 universities worldwide, offering rich dual-degree and exchange opportunities.

## 3. Faculties & Academic Strengths

Konkuk University comprises 10 diverse colleges. While all programs maintain high academic standards, three fields stand out as nationally renowned powerhouses:

* **College of Real Estate Sciences:** The first and most prestigious program of its kind in South Korea. It dominates the domestic real estate, urban planning, and development sectors.
* **College of Business Administration:** AACSB-accredited, offering a cutting-edge curriculum in finance, management, and global business.
* **College of Art & Design:** Highly competitive program known for producing elite designers, visual artists, and K-entertainment stars.

### TOPIK Scholarships for Freshmen
To attract top global talent, Konkuk reduces the financial burden through generous tuition waivers based on admission language scores:

* **TOPIK 6:** 100% Tuition Waiver for the first semester.
* **TOPIK 5:** 80% Tuition Waiver for the first semester.
* **TOPIK 4:** 60% Tuition Waiver for the first semester.
* **TOPIK 3:** 30% Tuition Waiver for the first semester.

For subsequent semesters, international students can receive 20% to 100% tuition waivers based on maintaining a competitive GPA (typically 3.0 or higher).

## 5. Admissions for International Students

Admissions are conducted twice a year for the Spring (March) and Fall (September) semesters.

### Key Requirements
* **Academic Eligibility:** Applicants and both parents must hold non-Korean citizenship. Applicants must have completed 12 years of elementary and secondary education.
* **Language Proficiency:** 
  * *Korean Track:* TOPIK Level 3 or higher (or completion of Level 3/4 at Konkuk KLI).
  * *English Track:* TOEFL iBT 80 or IELTS 5.5 or higher.
* **Required Documents:** High school graduation certificate, official transcripts, family relationship certificate, bank balance certificate (approx. USD 20,000), and a personal statement.

## 6. Campus Life & Location

The lifestyle at Konkuk is arguably one of its greatest selling points. 

* **Prime Location:** The campus sits directly next to **Konkuk University Station** (Seoul Subway Lines 2 and 7). This area is a thriving commercial hub, home to the Star City Mall, Lotte Department Store, and a bustling food street ("Rodeo Street") filled with trendy cafes, global restaurants, and nightlife spots.
* **On-Campus Housing (KU:L House):** This state-of-the-art dormitory complex can accommodate over 3,000 students. It features modern double rooms, card-key access, gyms, laundry facilities, and a cafeteria.
* **Campus Culture:** The scenic **Ilkamho Lake** provides a peaceful escape from busy city life. During spring and autumn festivals, students gather around the lake to enjoy live performances, food trucks, and outdoor activities.

## 7. FAQ

### Can I apply without knowing any Korean?
Yes. You can apply to pure English-track programs if you meet the TOEFL or IELTS requirements. Alternatively, you can enroll in the Konkuk Korean Language Institute (KLI) first, and then matriculate into a degree program once you reach TOPIK Level 3.

### What is the most famous major at Konkuk University?
The College of Real Estate Sciences is arguably the most famous and unique department, holding unmatched prestige in Korea. The College of Business Administration and the College of Art & Design are also highly sought-after.

### Is on-campus housing guaranteed for international students?
While housing is not 100% guaranteed, international freshmen are given high priority for spots in **KU:L House**, Konkuk's modern on-campus dormitory system.

### Are there part-time job opportunities for international students?
Yes. Students holding a D-2 student visa can legally work part-time (up to 20–25 hours per week depending on their Korean proficiency level) after obtaining permission from the university's International Office and the immigration authority.

### How much spending money do I need monthly?
Excluding tuition, a budget of KRW 800,000 to 1,200,000 per month is usually sufficient to cover housing, food, public transit, and moderate leisure activities in Seoul.
