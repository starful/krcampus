## School Overview
Established to foster global talent, the Sejong University Korean Language Center (KLC) provides systematic and professional Korean language training to international students. Located in the heart of Seoul (Gwangjin-gu), right next to the beautiful Children's Grand Park, the center offers an ideal learning environment with cutting-edge university facilities and a highly accessible subway connection (Line 7).

## Curriculum & Academic Structure
The Regular Program runs on a 4-semester-per-year system (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter). Each semester lasts for 10 weeks, totaling 200 hours of intensive study. 
- **Levels 1 to 2 (Beginner):** Focuses on basic survival Korean, everyday conversation, pronunciation, and fundamental grammar.
- **Levels 3 to 4 (Intermediate):** Develops fluency in social situations, expression of thoughts on familiar topics, and core grammar for daily life in Korea.
- **Levels 5 to 6 (Advanced):** Focuses on academic, professional, and abstract topics, preparing students for university lectures and professional work environments.

## Features & Cultural Programs
- **TOPIK Special Classes:** In-depth preparation lectures are offered to help students achieve high TOPIK scores, which are essential for university admissions.
- **Cultural Activities:** Every semester, the center organizes cultural excursions including historical site visits, traditional pottery making, K-Pop dance classes, and Taekwondo experiences.
- **Language Exchange Partner Program:** KLC matches international students with Korean undergraduate students at Sejong University to promote natural communication and peer-to-peer mentoring.

## Accommodation & Campus Facilities
International language students are eligible to apply for the modern on-campus **Sejong University Happy Dormitory**. The dormitory features double rooms equipped with desks, wardrobes, air conditioning, and high-speed Wi-Fi. Common facilities include laundry rooms, study lounges, a fitness center, and a convenience store.

## Pathway to Degree Programs
Students who successfully complete Level 4 or higher at the Sejong University Korean Language Center are eligible for admission into Sejong University's undergraduate programs. Furthermore, the university offers generous tuition waiver scholarships (up to 100%) for students transitioning from the language center to undergraduate or graduate programs based on their TOPIK proficiency.
