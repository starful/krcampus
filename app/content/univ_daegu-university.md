## University Overview

Established in 1956, **Daegu University** is a highly respected private university located in the Gyeongsan suburbs of the Daegu Metropolitan Area. Rooted in the founding principles of "Love, Light, and Freedom," the university has built a stellar national reputation for its commitment to social welfare, special education, and rehabilitation science. 

With a massive, beautiful green campus that is widely considered one of the largest and most scenic in South Korea, Daegu University offers an ideal, peaceful environment for academic focus. It serves as a vibrant educational hub for over 20,000 domestic students and a rapidly growing population of over 1,300 international students from dozens of countries.

---

## Academic Strengths and Specialized Fields

Daegu University is uniquely distinguished in South Korea for its pioneering programs in human welfare and specialized care:

*   **Special Education & Rehabilitation Science:** Daegu University is widely acknowledged as the absolute pioneer and national leader in these fields. It was the first university in South Korea to establish a College of Rehabilitation Science, and its Special Education programs produce a significant percentage of the country's specialized educators.
*   **Information Technology & Engineering:** The College of Information and Communication Engineering adapts rapidly to industry trends, offering strong pathways in software development, AI, and smart vehicle technologies.
*   **Business and Social Sciences:** The Business Administration and Social Welfare programs are highly practical, offering domestic and global internship opportunities designed to boost graduate employability.

---

## Generous Scholarships for International Students

Daegu University offers an exceptionally supportive tuition relief structure to attract global talent. Foreign students can significantly reduce their financial burden through TOPIK-based and academic merit scholarships:

*   **Admission Scholarship:** Based on the applicant's Korean proficiency (TOPIK level), tuition discounts ranging from **30% to 80% (or even 100% for TOPIK Level 6)** are applied to the first semester.
*   **Academic Progression Scholarship:** From the second semester onward, students who maintain a competitive GPA can continue to receive **30% to 70% tuition waivers**.
*   **Korean Language Program Pathways:** Students transitioning from Daegu University's own Korean Language Education Center to an undergraduate degree are often awarded exclusive admission fee waivers and transition bonuses.

## Campus Life and International Support

### Accommodation & Living
Daegu University features excellent, modern on-campus housing (Biho Dormitory complex) with dedicated rooms for international students. The dormitories are highly affordable compared to accommodations in Seoul, featuring gyms, convenience stores, study rooms, and multi-faith prayer rooms. 

### Student Welfare & Cultural Integration
The **Office of International Affairs** provides comprehensive support services, including alien registration assistance, health insurance guidance, and academic counseling. Additionally, the university organizes various cultural exchange programs, such as:
*   **The Global Buddy Program:** Pairing international students with domestic Korean students to ease adjustment to campus life and practice Korean language skills.
*   **Cultural Field Trips:** University-sponsored excursions to historic Korean cities like Gyeongju, Busan, and Seoul.
*   **International Food Festivals:** Celebrating the global diversity of the campus community.

## Why Choose Daegu University?

1.  **Affordable Cost of Living:** Located just outside Daegu, Gyeongsan offers the benefits of a major metropolitan area with significantly lower rent, food, and living costs than Seoul.
2.  **Unrivaled Campus Facilities:** The campus features extensive walking trails, a beautiful lake (Muncheonji), world-class sports facilities, and high-tech libraries.
3.  **A True Global Community:** With dedicated international support systems and structured Korean language pathways, students from all backgrounds can smoothly transition into the South Korean academic and professional landscapes.
