## School Overview
Established in 1970, the **Kyung Hee University Institute of International Education (IIE)** is one of South Korea's premier and most historic language institutes. Located on Kyung Hee University's stunning, Gothic-style Seoul campus, the IIE welcomes thousands of international students from over 100 countries annually. 

Kyung Hee University is internationally acclaimed for its humanities education, and its language school has crafted a world-class curriculum that emphasizes communication skills, cultural appreciation, and academic excellence.

## Why Choose Kyung Hee University IIE?
* **A Breath-Taking Campus Environment:** Famous for its historic architecture, blooming cherry blossoms, and park-like grounds, Kyung Hee provides an inspiring setting for academic focus and student life.
* **Integrated Language and Culture Curriculum:** Rather than just memorizing vocabulary, students actively experience Korea through events like Korean Speech Contests, K-Pop dance classes, and historical excursions.
* **Pathway to Degree Programs:** As one of Korea's top-tier universities, Kyung Hee IIE provides direct counselling, information sessions, and TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) preparation programs for students wishing to advance to undergraduate or graduate schools in Korea.
* **Global Network & Buddy Program:** The school connects international students with domestic Kyung Hee students through its vibrant 'DOU-UMI' (Buddy Program) to help newcomers adjust to life in Seoul and practice their Korean naturally.

## Curriculum & Programs

### 1. Regular Program (10 Weeks)
This is the flagship program designed for students who want to build systematic academic and professional proficiency in Korean.
* **Structure:** 4 semesters per year (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter). Each semester lasts 10 weeks (200 hours of instruction total).
* **Schedule:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM (Morning class) or 1:30 PM – 5:30 PM (Afternoon class).
* **Levels Offered:** Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Advanced).
* **Key Subjects:** Speaking, listening, reading, writing, and grammar, with customized multimedia materials.

### 2. Short-Term Program (3 Weeks)
Perfect for students, tourists, and professionals looking to experience intensive Korean learning during their vacation.
* **Structure:** 15 days of instruction combining daily language learning with cultural field trips in the afternoon.
* **Highlights:** Cultural experiences such as Taekwondo, Korean cooking classes, and visits to major tourist attractions in Seoul.

## Campus Life & Accommodations
* **Dormitories (Woo Jung Won & On-Campus Housing):** International students have access to modern university dorms equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, laundry rooms, study lounges, and gym facilities.
* **Campus Amenities:** Students have access to the Central Library, student cafeterias, health clinics, and extensive sports facilities.
* **Location:** Located in Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul, Kyung Hee is highly accessible via public transport (Hoegi Station on Subway Line 1 and the Gyeongui-Jungang Line), allowing students to easily explore famous nearby areas like Hongdae, Gangnam, and Myeongdong.
