## School Overview
Founded in 2002, the **Hongik University International Language Institute (국제언어교육원)** has established itself as a premier institution for Korean language and cultural education. Located in Mapo-gu, Seoul, it lies in the heart of Hongdae—Korea's most famous district for youth culture, street performances, indie music, and contemporary arts. This dynamic setting offers international students an unparalleled environment to practice Korean in real-world scenarios while experiencing the vanguard of modern Korean wave (Hallyu) culture.

## Curriculum & Programs

### 1. Regular Program (10-Week Semesters)
The core program is structured around four 10-week terms per year (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter). It runs for 200 hours per semester (Monday to Friday, 4 hours per day).
- **Levels**: 1 to 6 (Beginner to Advanced).
- **Class Size**: Average of 12-15 students per class to maximize speaking time and individual attention.
- **Focus Areas**: Integrated practice of Speaking, Listening, Reading, and Writing, alongside practical communication skills designed for daily life and academic pursuits.

### 2. Short-Term Program
During the summer and winter vacations, Hongik University offers intensive short-term courses designed for students who want to improve their Korean language skills while participating in diverse cultural workshops over a 3-week period.

## Outstanding Features
- **The Ultimate Location**: Hongdae is legendary for its artistic flair. Students can step outside the classroom and instantly access trendy cafés, indie design shops, live music clubs, and vibrant street markets.
- **Cultural Excursions**: Every term features curated cultural excursions, such as traditional Korean pottery making, K-Pop dance classes, cooking traditional food, and visiting historic landmarks.
- **Pathway to Higher Education**: Hongik University is world-renowned for its Fine Arts and Design programs. The Language Institute offers specialized guidance, admission consultations, and tuition discounts for language students transitioning to undergraduate or graduate programs at Hongik.
- **Dormitory Accommodations**: International students have the opportunity to apply for safe, clean, and highly affordable on-campus housing (Je2-Haksa) that features modern amenities and 24/7 security.
