## School Overview
Established in 1986, the **Korea University Korean Language Center (KLC)** is one of the most prestigious language institutes in South Korea. Korea University itself is a member of the elite "SKY" universities, representing the pinnacle of higher education in Korea. 

The KLC has educated over 90,000 students from more than 120 countries. Located in Seongbuk-gu, Seoul, the campus boasts iconic European-style gothic architecture and a vibrant, student-friendly neighborhood in Anam-dong.

## Curriculum & Programs

### 1. Regular Program (10 Weeks)
This is the flagship course designed for students seeking a systematic and thorough understanding of the Korean language for academic, professional, or personal reasons.
- **Duration:** 10 weeks per semester (200 hours total).
- **Schedule:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM (Morning class) or 1:45 PM - 5:45 PM (Afternoon class).
- **Levels:** Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Advanced).
- **Focus:** Balanced training in Speaking, Listening, Reading, and Writing, with a strong emphasis on active communication.

### 2. Short-Term Intensive Program (3 Weeks)
Designed for students who wish to improve their Korean language skills and experience Korean culture during their vacation.
- **Duration:** 3 weeks (60 hours total).
- **Schedule:** Intensive daily language classes combined with special afternoon cultural activities.

## Special Features & Student Support

- **KU Buddy Program:** Match with current undergraduate students of Korea University to practice conversational Korean, make friends, and quickly adapt to life in Seoul.
- **Academic & University Pathways:** The KLC provides regular counseling and admission information sessions for students aiming to enter undergraduate or graduate programs at Korea University.
- **Excellent Facilities:** KLC students have access to Korea University's main libraries, fitness centers, and various student support lounges.
- **Cultural Activities:** Every term, KLC organizes cultural excursions, including Korean traditional cooking classes, K-Pop dance workshops, and historical palace tours.

## Accommodation (Dormitories)
Korea University offers comfortable on-campus housing options for international language students, including:
- **CJ International House & Anam Global House:** Semi-private or shared rooms equipped with modern amenities, study rooms, fitness facilities, and high-speed internet. 
- *Note: Due to high demand, dormitory spots are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, and students are advised to apply as early as possible.*

## Application Requirements
To apply for the Regular Program, applicants must submit:
1. Completed Online Application Form.
2. Graduation Certificate or Diploma of the last school attended (apostilled or consular-verified).
3. Copy of Passport.
4. Bank Balance Certificate showing a minimum of $10,000 USD (or equivalent) for visa processing.
5. Application Fee (Non-refundable): 60,000 KRW.
