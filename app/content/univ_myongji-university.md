## University Overview

Established in 1948, **Myongji University (명지대학교)** is a prestigious private Christian institution located in South Korea. Known for its academic excellence, global diversity, and strong moral foundation, Myongji has built a reputation as a welcoming haven for international students. 

With two highly specialized campuses, Myongji provides an optimized learning environment for students in various disciplines. The university maintains robust international networks, helping students transition seamlessly from academics to global careers.

---

## The Dual Campus System

Myongji University operates a unique, specialized dual-campus structure divided by academic discipline:

1. **Seoul Campus (Humanities and Social Sciences):** Located in Seodaemun-gu, Seoul, this campus is nestled in a vibrant cultural district close to major universities like Yonsei and Sogang. It houses the Colleges of Humanities, Social Sciences, Business Administration, Law, and ICT Convergence.
2. **Natural Science Campus (Yongin):** Located in the nearby suburban city of Yongin (accessible by public transport from Seoul), this beautiful, spacious campus houses the Colleges of Natural Sciences, Engineering, Arts and Physical Education, and Architecture.

---

## Academic Strength and Top Programs

Myongji is well-regarded across several academic disciplines, especially recognized for:

* **College of Architecture:** Highly competitive, offering a 5-year professional program that is fully accredited internationally (KAAB). It is widely regarded as one of the best architecture schools in South Korea.
* **Business Administration:** Fully accredited program focusing on global supply chains, international trade, and hands-on business modeling.
* **College of ICT Convergence:** A forward-looking faculty preparing students for careers in AI, software engineering, and digital media.
* **Korean Language Institute (KLI):** Myongji's language center is famous for its intensive Korean programs, cultural immersion classes, and high success rates in helping students achieve TOPIK Level 3 or higher for university admission.

---

## Support Systems for International Students

Myongji University prides itself on being highly accessible and supportive of its global student body. Key support features include:

* **The OIA (Office of International Affairs):** A dedicated administrative office assisting students with visas, health insurance, course registrations, and daily living adjustments.
* **Myongji Global Buddy Program:** Pairs incoming international students with local Korean students to ease cultural transitions, practice language skills, and explore Seoul together.
* **Generous Scholarships:** International students are eligible for merit-based tuition waivers ranging from 30% to 100% based on their GPA and Korean/English proficiency test scores (TOPIK, TOEFL, IELTS).
* **Modern On-Campus Dormitories:** Both the Seoul and Yongin campuses offer clean, secure, and modern residential facilities equipped with gyms, study rooms, and high-speed internet.

---

## Admission Guide for International Students

To apply for undergraduate admission at Myongji, international candidates must typically fulfill the following requirements:

1. **Language Proficiency:**
   * **Korean Track:** Minimum TOPIK Level 3 (or Level 4 for transfer students). Alternatively, completion of Intermediate 1 level at Myongji's own Korean Language Institute.
   * **English Track:** Minimum IELTS 5.5, TOEFL iBT 80, or equivalent standard test scores (applicable for English-taught majors).
2. **Academic Credentials:** Graduation certificate and official transcripts from an accredited high school (or university for transfer students), legalized by apostille or consular verification.
3. **Financial Capacity:** Proof of sufficient financial capacity (typically a bank balance certificate showing at least $16,000 USD, in compliance with South Korean immigration guidelines).
