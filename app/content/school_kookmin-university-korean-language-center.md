## School Overview
Kookmin University's Korean Language Center (KLC), located in Seongbuk-gu, Seoul, is a prestigious educational institution dedicated to teaching the Korean language and culture to international students. Surrounded by the beautiful Bukhansan National Park, the campus offers a peaceful, clean, and highly focused environment for study while remaining well-connected to the vibrant lifestyle of downtown Seoul.

Since its establishment, the center has welcomed thousands of students from all over the world, helping them achieve their academic, professional, and personal language goals through high-quality instruction and immersive activities.

## Why Choose Kookmin University KLC?
* **Outstanding Faculty:** Courses are taught by professional, highly experienced native instructors holding specialized qualifications in Teaching Korean as a Foreign Language.
* **Communication-Focused Curriculum:** The program focuses on practical, real-life communication, integrating speaking, listening, reading, and writing naturally.
* **Comprehensive Cultural Experience:** Every semester, students can participate in exciting cultural excursions, such as visiting historic sites, making traditional Korean crafts, and joining K-wave experiences.
* **Great Campus Amenities:** Language students gain full access to university facilities, including the library, dynamic student lounges, sports facilities, and affordable cafeterias.
* **Convenient Accommodation:** Kookmin provides on-campus housing options that are safe, modern, and highly accessible, helping newcomers settle into South Korea comfortably.

## Program Details
### Regular Program (10 Weeks)
The flagship program runs four times a year in spring, summer, autumn, and winter. It spans 10 weeks per term for a total of 200 hours of intensive classroom instruction.
* **Levels 1-2 (Beginner):** Mastering Hangul, basic everyday greetings, ordering food, and navigating public transportation.
* **Levels 3-4 (Intermediate):** Transitioning to professional and social topics, expressions of opinion, and preparing for the mid-level TOPIK exams.
* **Levels 5-6 (Advanced):** Academic research language, advanced reading comprehension, professional vocabulary, and debating contemporary social issues.

### Short-term Program (3 Weeks)
Ideal for those who want to experience intensive language learning combined with cultural excursions during their winter or summer vacations.

## Admission & Visa Support
Applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent qualification. The center provides systematic support for securing a D-4-1 (General Trainee) student visa for qualified applicants registering for two semesters or more.
