## University Overview
Established in 1947 as the first private university after Korea's liberation, Dankook University (DKU) has historically stood as a beacon of academic excellence, patriotism, and self-reliance. Over the decades, it has evolved into a highly respected comprehensive research university. 

Dankook operates a unique "One University, Two Campuses" system. The Jukjeon Campus, located in Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do, serves as the main hub focusing on Information Technology (IT), Culture Technology (CT), and traditional humanities. The Cheonan Campus focuses on Biotechnology (BT), medicine, and dentistry. For international students, the Jukjeon Campus offers a scenic, modern suburban experience with incredibly close proximity to Seoul's metropolitan transit system.

## Academic Specializations & Strengths
Dankook University is a powerhouse in technology, business, and arts. Key academic strengths include:
* **College of Software Science & Engineering:** Highly regarded for its curriculum in Computer Science, Mobile System Engineering, and Cybersecurity, benefiting from partnerships with neighboring technology hubs in Pangyo and Bundang.
* **School of Business and Economics:** Offers dynamic global business tracks and active internship placement programs.
* **College of Music and Arts:** Renowned for producing some of Korea's top actors, musicians, and designers, making it a dream destination for students interested in Hallyu (Korean Wave) culture.

## Campus Life & Location (Gyeonggi-do)
The Jukjeon Campus is beautifully situated on the slopes of Mount Beophwa in Yongin-si, Gyeonggi-do. It offers a peaceful, green environment conducive to studying, while remaining highly accessible to metropolitan Seoul via the Bundang Subway Line (Jukjeon Station). Students can easily commute to popular areas like Gangnam within 40 minutes. 

The campus boasts top-tier facilities including the Toegye Memorial Library, modern research laboratories, performing arts halls, and comprehensive athletic complexes.

## Admissions & Support for International Students
With a diverse community of over 2,200 international students, Dankook University provides robust support networks designed to ensure quick adaptation:
* **Global Education Center:** Provides academic counseling, immigration guidance, and visa support.
* **DKU Buddy Program:** Pairs international freshmen with domestic students to facilitate cultural exchange and campus integration.
* **Korean Language Program:** Offers high-quality, intensive language preparation tracks for students aiming to matriculate into Korean-taught undergraduate or graduate programs.

## Scholarships & Financial Aid
Dankook University offers generous scholarship packages for international students. Outstanding applicants can receive tuition waivers ranging from 30% to 100% based on their TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) scores or English proficiency test scores (such as TOEFL or IELTS) upon admission. Furthermore, GPA-based academic excellence scholarships are available for enrolled students in subsequent semesters.
