## Introduction

Hankuk University of Foreign Studies (HUFS), established in 1954, is South Korea's premier institution for foreign languages, international relations, and global studies. Renowned for its motto, "Truth, Peace, and Creation," HUFS has played a pivotal role in connecting South Korea to the international stage post-war, consistently producing top-tier diplomats, international business leaders, and world-class translators.

## Academic Excellence & Specialized Tracks

HUFS is highly regarded for its unique curriculum that seamlessly integrates linguistic expertise with humanities, social sciences, and international business:

- **Division of International Studies (DIS):** A highly selective undergraduate program taught entirely in English. It focuses on international relations, international law, and global economics.
- **Language & Diplomacy (LD) & Language & Technology (LT):** Specialized elite tracks designed to groom future diplomats and computational linguists, offering intensive training and generous scholarship incentives.
- **Unmatched Language Portfolios:** HUFS teaches over 45 foreign languages, making it one of the top language-specialized universities globally.

## Global Network & Exchange Programs

With active exchange partnerships with over 800 universities in nearly 100 countries, HUFS offers unparalleled global mobility. The famous **'7+1 Study Abroad Program'** allows students to study at least one of their eight semesters at an overseas partner university, supported by university-sponsored scholarship options.

## Campus Life & Seoul Location

Located in Dongdaemun-gu in northeastern Seoul, the main campus is vibrant, highly accessible, and compact. It features modern learning commons, global lounges, and a rich array of international student clubs. International students enjoy a highly supportive atmosphere, with dedicated organizations like the **International Student Organization (ISO)** assisting with cultural adjustments and peer mentoring.

## Career Prospects for International Graduates

Graduates of HUFS are highly sought after by multinational corporations, foreign embassies, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and trade agencies. The university’s strong emphasis on multilingualism, combined with an understanding of global market dynamics, gives international students a distinct advantage in both the Korean and international job markets.
