## University Overview

Founded with the vision to become a world-leading science and technology university, the **Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST)** is one of South Korea's premier research-oriented public universities. Along with KAIST, GIST, and DGIST, UNIST is recognized as one of the country's four specialized institutes dedicated to advancing science and technology.

Located in **Ulsan**, the industrial powerhouse of South Korea, UNIST serves as a crucial hub for academic-industrial cooperation. The university has quickly risen in global rankings due to its high-impact research outputs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a highly international academic environment.

---

## Why International Students Choose UNIST

### 1. 100% Lectures in English
Unlike many Korean universities where only a fraction of classes are taught in English, UNIST stands out by conducting **100% of its undergraduate and graduate lectures in English**. This makes it an ideal choice for global students who wish to study in Korea without a prior mastery of the Korean language.

### 2. Exceptional Financial Support
UNIST offers some of the most generous scholarship programs in Asia. Most admitted international students receive:
* **Full Tuition Waivers** for the entire duration of their degree (provided they maintain a minimum GPA).
* **Monthly Living Stipends** to cover food and basic personal expenses.
* **Guaranteed Dormitory Housing** at highly subsidized rates.

### 3. World-Class Research & Innovation
UNIST is highly ranked globally for its research citations, particularly in fields like **secondary batteries (batteries), materials science, chemical engineering, and biotechnology**. Students have direct access to cutting-edge research facilities, including the UNIST Central Research Facilities (UCRF), which house advanced electron microscopes, cleanrooms, and nanofabrication equipment.

---

## Academic Programs and Departments

UNIST focuses heavily on STEM disciplines, organized into highly interdisciplinary schools rather than rigid traditional departments:

* **Engineering & Physical Sciences**: Focuses on advanced manufacturing, materials, nuclear engineering, and environmental technologies.
* **Information & Computer Sciences**: Specializes in Artificial Intelligence, big data, robotics, and electrical engineering.
* **Natural Sciences**: Excellent programs in advanced physics, chemistry, and mathematical sciences.
* **Life Sciences & Biomedical Engineering**: Focuses on genomic medicine, bio-chips, and brain sciences.
* **Business Administration**: Combines technical knowledge with management strategies, specifically designed for technology-driven enterprises.

---

## Campus Life and Support Systems

### Accommodation
All international students are guaranteed housing in the on-campus dormitories. The residences are modern, equipped with high-speed internet, study rooms, gym facilities, and convenience stores. Special international kitchens are available for students to cook their own meals.

### Global Center
The **UNIST Global Center** provides comprehensive support for international scholars and students. From visa counseling, alien registration, and health insurance registration to organizing cultural excursion programs, the center ensures a smooth transition to life in Korea.

### Location: Ulsan
Ulsan is Korea’s industrial capital, home to massive complexes operated by **Hyundai Motor Company, Hyundai Heavy Industries, and SK Energy**. This geographical advantage provides students with unparalleled opportunities for internships, industrial field trips, and post-graduation employment in world-leading manufacturing and energy sectors.
