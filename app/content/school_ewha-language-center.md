## School Overview
Established in 1962, the **Ewha Language Center (ELC)** at Ewha Womans University is one of Korea's premier institutions for Korean language education. Located in the vibrant Seodaemun-gu district of Seoul, ELC attracts thousands of students from over 80 countries annually. 

One of the most common misconceptions is that the language center is female-only. While Ewha Womans University is a women's university at the undergraduate level, **the Ewha Language Center is co-educational** and welcomes students of all genders. 

ELC is globally renowned for its communication-centered educational philosophy and its self-developed textbook series, *'Ewha Korean'*, which is widely used by Korean learners worldwide.

## Why Choose Ewha Language Center?

*   **Speaking & Communication Focus:** Unlike traditional programs that heavily emphasize grammar drills, Ewha's curriculum focuses on speaking, conversational fluency, and practical everyday Korean.
*   **The World-Class 'Ewha Korean' Textbooks:** Students learn directly from the authors of these highly acclaimed books, ensuring a seamless and high-quality learning curve.
*   **Breathtaking Campus and ECC:** Students have access to Ewha's stunning campus, including the iconic Ewha Campus Complex (ECC), which houses study areas, cafes, a cinema, and fitness centers.
*   **Ewha Buddy Program:** International students are matched with Ewha undergraduate students, allowing for natural language exchange, cultural sharing, and fast adaptation to life in Seoul.

## Curriculum & Course Options

### 1. Regular Program (Intensive 10-Week)
This program is designed for students seeking academic proficiency, career development, or long-term language acquisition.
*   **Schedule:** 10 weeks, 200 hours total (Monday to Friday, 4 hours/day).
*   **Levels:** Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Advanced).
*   **Vibe:** Rigorous but highly communicative. Includes cultural activities such as cooking classes, traditional craft making, or watching non-verbal performances.

### 2. Short-Term Program (3-Week)
Perfect for travelers, students on university breaks, or those who want a taste of Korean language and culture.
*   **Schedule:** 3 weeks, 60 hours total.
*   **Focus:** Practical conversational Korean combined with afternoon cultural excursions around Seoul.

## Campus Life & Accommodation

### Accommodation (Dormitories)
ELC students can apply for the university's on-campus dormitories, which are modern, safe, and conveniently located. 
*   **Dormitory Options:** International House (single/double rooms) and E-House (apartment-style unit rooms).
*   **Facilities:** High-speed Wi-Fi, laundry rooms, study lounges, convenience stores, and gym access.
*   *Note: Due to high demand, housing is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.* 

### Student Life
The neighborhood surrounding Ewha Womans University is famous for its affordable shopping, trendy cafes, and diverse dining options. It is also adjacent to Sinchon and Yonsei University, creating a massive, youthful student hub perfect for socializing and networking with domestic and international peers.
