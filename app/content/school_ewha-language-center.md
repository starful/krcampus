## 1. School Overview
Established in 1962, the Ewha Language Center (ELC) at Ewha Womans University is a premier Korean language training institute located in Seodaemun-gu, Seoul. Catering to over 2,500 students annually, ELC is globally recognized for its highly acclaimed "Ewha Korean" textbook series and its speaking-focused curriculum.

* **Address:** Ewha Womans University, 52 Ewhayeodae-gil, Seodaemun-gu, Seoul, South Korea
* **Co-educational Status:** Although Ewha is a women's university, the ELC programs are fully **co-educational**. Male students are welcome to enroll in all language programs.

## 2. Programs & Schedule
The ELC offers three main academic tracks designed for various learner goals, ranging from academic preparation to light conversational study.

### Program Schedule & Structure
| Program Name | Levels | Duration | Hours/Week | Class Schedule | Admission Months |

| **Intensive Program (10-Week)** | Levels 1–6 | 10 weeks (200 hrs) | 20 hours | Mon–Fri, 09:00–13:00 or 14:00–18:00 | March, June, September, December |
| **Evening Program (10-Week)** | Levels 1–6 | 10 weeks (60 hrs) | 6 hours | Mon, Tue, Thu, 19:00–21:00 | March, September |
| **Short-Term Program (3-Week)** | Levels 1–5 | 3 weeks (60 hrs) | 20 hours | Mon–Fri, 09:00–13:00 | February, August |

* **Intensive Program:** Focuses heavily on academic and professional Korean, balanced across speaking, listening, reading, and writing.
* **Evening Program:** Geared towards working professionals or students busy during the day, focusing on practical communication.
* **Short-Term Program:** Concentrated study combined with cultural activities, ideal for vacation periods.

## 4. Admissions & D-4 Visa Steps
International students wishing to study for more than one term (20 weeks) in the Intensive Program generally require a D-4 (General Trainee) visa.

### Step-by-Step Application Process
1. **Online Application:** Register on the ELC website and upload digital copies of required documents.
2. **Document Review:** The ELC admission board evaluates academic credentials and financial stability.
3. **Invoice & Payment:** Approved applicants receive a tuition invoice. Transfer the total fees via international bank transfer.
4. **Standard Admission Certificate (CoA):** ELC issues the CoA once payment is confirmed.
5. **Visa Application:** Submit the CoA along with other documents to the nearest Korean Embassy or Consulate.

### Required Documents
* Completed online application form.
* Copy of passport identification page.
* Graduation certificate or enrollment certificate from the highest level of education (apostilled or consular-verified).
* Bank balance certificate showing at least USD 10,000 (or equivalent) held for over a month.
* One passport-sized photo.

## 5. TOPIK & University Pathway
ELC utilizes its specialized "Ewha Korean" curriculum, which integrates TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) preparation into regular coursework.
* **TOPIK Preparation:** Regular classes emphasize grammar patterns and vocabulary common in TOPIK I and II exams. Level-completion tests mimic TOPIK formats.
* **University Pathways:** Ewha Womans University offers admission advantages to students who complete ELC courses. Students who complete Level 3 or higher satisfy the language requirement for undergraduate admission, while Level 4 or higher is standard for graduate admissions.
* **Scholarships:** ELC graduates transitioning to undergraduate or graduate programs at Ewha may qualify for specific linguistic capability scholarships.

## 6. Dormitory & Living in the City
* **On-Campus Accommodation:** Language students can apply for the International House (I-House) or Hanwoori Hall. Rooms are configured as singles or doubles. Demand is high, so early application is mandatory.
* **Ewha Global Buddy Program:** This program pairs international language students with current Ewha undergraduate students. It provides peer-to-peer language exchange, cultural outings, and campus adjustment support.
* **Sinchon District:** The university is located in Sinchon, one of Seoul's most active student neighborhoods. It features cheap dining, cafes, and easy access to Line 2 (Ewha Womans University Station), connecting students to the rest of Seoul.

## 7. FAQ

### Can male students apply to the Ewha Language Center?
Yes. While the undergraduate and graduate divisions of Ewha Womans University are strictly for women, the Ewha Language Center is fully co-educational and welcomes male students of all nationalities.

### How do I take the placement test?
New students with prior knowledge of Korean must take a mandatory placement test (written and oral) before classes begin. Complete beginners start automatically at Level 1 without a test.

### Is on-campus housing guaranteed for language students?
No. Dormitory spaces are limited and distributed on a first-come, first-served basis. If you do not secure a room, you must search for off-campus options like Goshiwons, Share Houses, or Studio apartments (One-rooms) in the Sinchon area.

### Can I get a refund if my D-4 visa is denied?
Yes. If your visa is officially denied by the Korean embassy, you must submit a refund application along with the official visa rejection letter. ELC will refund the tuition fee, excluding the non-refundable registration fee.

### Can I work part-time while studying on a D-4 visa?
Yes, but only after completing six months (one semester) of continuous study with at least a 90% attendance record and maintaining satisfactory grades. You must obtain formal permission from both the school and the Immigration Office.
