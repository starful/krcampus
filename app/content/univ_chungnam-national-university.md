## University Overview
Chungnam National University (CNU), established in 1952, is one of the premier Flagship Korean National Universities (KNU). Located in Daejeon, the scientific and technological heart of South Korea, CNU has played a pivotal role in training elite researchers, engineers, and regional leaders. The university is widely celebrated for its academic rigor, comprehensive research facilities, and its commitment to making high-quality global higher education accessible through affordable state-subsidized tuition.

## Academic Excellence & Industry Integration
CNU is highly regarded for its STEM, agricultural, and biomedical disciplines. Because Daejeon is home to **Daedeok Innopolis**—a high-tech district containing over 250 research institutes, corporate R&D centers, and venture firms—CNU students enjoy unparalleled access to internships, collaborative research opportunities, and career placement options. Key strengths include:
- **Engineering & IT**: Strongly integrated with national research labs.
- **Agriculture and Life Sciences**: One of the most historically significant and well-funded agricultural colleges in Korea.
- **Veterinary Medicine & Pharmacy**: Highly competitive programs recognized nationwide.

## Tuition and Scholarships
As a national institution, CNU offers a distinct economic advantage for international students:
- **Low Tuition**: Standard humanities tuition begins at approximately 1,700,000 KRW (~$1,300 USD) per semester, while engineering and lab-based majors cost around 2,400,000 KRW (~$1,800 USD) per semester.
- **Generous Scholarships**: 
  - **Freshmen Scholarships**: Tuition waivers of 40% up to 100% are awarded based on entry TOPIK scores (typically requiring TOPIK Level 4, 5, or 6).
  - **Academic Progress Scholarships**: GPA-based tuition waivers are available for subsequent semesters to students maintaining a solid academic standing.

## Campus Life and Location Benefits
CNU's main **Daedong Campus** in Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, is spacious, flat, and integrated with a lively college town atmosphere. 
- **Daejeon Advantage**: As South Korea's fifth-largest metropolis, Daejeon offers a perfect balance of urban amenities and lower living costs compared to Seoul. It is also the central transport hub of Korea; Seoul is reachable in just 50 minutes via the KTX high-speed train.
- **Global Community**: The university features dedicated international dormitories, global lounges, and robust peer-mentoring (buddy) programs to help incoming students transition smoothly to life in Korea.

## Admissions Strategy for International Students
While English-tracked programs exist primarily at the graduate level, the undergraduate division heavily favors proficiency in the Korean language. 
- **TOPIK Requirement**: An official TOPIK level of 3 or higher is typically mandatory for general admission, though aiming for Level 4 or higher is strongly recommended to secure initial tuition waivers and thrive in lectures.
- **Language Academy Pathway**: Many international students enroll in CNU’s Korean Language Education Center first, which offers a highly systematic curriculum and direct assistance preparing for the university's degree programs.
