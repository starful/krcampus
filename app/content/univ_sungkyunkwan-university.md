## University Overview
Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU) is a world-class institution with a deep historical foundation. Established in 1398 as the supreme national educational institution of the Joseon Dynasty, SKKU seamlessly bridges Korea’s rich traditional heritage with cutting-edge modern research. Backed heavily by the global conglomerate Samsung since 1996, SKKU has undergone rapid internationalization, modernizing its facilities, academic curriculum, and research capabilities to rank among the top universities globally.

## Campus Structure & Living
SKKU operates a dual-campus system tailored to academic focus areas:
- **Humanities and Social Sciences Campus (Seoul):** Situated in the historic heart of Seoul (Hyehwa/Jongno district), this campus houses majors such as Business, Economics, Social Sciences, and Humanities. It sits adjacent to Changgyeonggung Palace, offering a beautiful mix of traditional Hanok architecture and state-of-the-art facilities.
- **Natural Sciences Campus (Suwon):** Located just south of Seoul in Suwon (easily accessible via subway Line 1), this campus is a hub for Engineering, Medicine, Software, and Natural Sciences. It features world-class research facilities funded by Samsung and houses the iconic, modern Samsung Library.

## Special Advantages for International Students
1. **The Samsung Partnership:** SKKU's deep-rooted connection with Samsung translates directly into benefits for students. This partnership supports specialized programs (such as the Global Business and Global Economics tracks), offers robust industry-academia internships, and provides pathways to recruitment opportunities with global tech and business leaders.
2. **Extensive English-Track Programs:** Recognizing the needs of global students, SKKU offers a broad spectrum of English-taught courses. Prestigious programs like the Global Business Administration (GBA) and Global Economics are taught entirely in English by international faculty.
3. **Generous Global Scholarships:** SKKU offers generous financial aid packages for incoming international students, ranging from 10% to 100% tuition waivers based on admission evaluation scores, as well as academic excellence scholarships for enrolled students based on GPA.

## Admissions & Application Tips
Admission to SKKU is competitive. Key factors for a successful application include:
- **Academic Performance:** A strong high school GPA or prior college transcripts are crucial.
- **Language Proficiency:** For English-track programs, a strong IELTS (6.0+) or TOEFL (iBT 80+) score is necessary. For Korean-track programs, TOPIK Level 4 or higher is highly recommended to ensure academic integration.
- **Personal Statement & Study Plan:** Clearly articulating why you want to study at SKKU, detailing how your career goals align with the specific major, and showcasing your adaptability to Korean culture will heavily influence the admissions committee.
