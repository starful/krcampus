## School Overview
Established to foster global talent and promote the Korean language, the **Dankook University (DKU) Korean Language Center** at the Jukjeon Campus in Yongin provides a highly systematic language curriculum. Dankook University is widely recognized for its beautiful, modern campus and outstanding academic reputation. The Language Center offers international students a perfect environment to achieve Korean proficiency while transitioning smoothly into undergraduate or graduate studies.

## Program Curriculum
Our Regular Program is divided into four 10-week terms per year (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter). 
* **Class Schedule**: Monday to Friday (5 days a week), 4 hours per day (either morning session 09:00 - 13:00 or afternoon session 13:30 - 17:30).
* **Levels Offered**: Levels 1 to 6 (Beginner to Advanced). 
* **Focus Areas**: Integrated practice in speaking, listening, reading, and writing, with an emphasis on real-world communication and targeted training for the Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK).

## Special Features & Student Support
* **University Pathways**: Graduates of the language program who achieve TOPIK Level 3 or higher receive prioritized consideration and special admission pathways for undergraduate majors at Dankook University.
* **Dormitory Priority**: On-campus housing (Woongbi Hall) is available for foreign language students, offering modern amenities, high-speed internet, card-key security, and convenient dining options.
* **Cultural Programs**: Each semester, the center hosts immersive cultural events, including K-pop dance classes, pottery crafting, historical site visits (such as Gyeongbokgung Palace and Folk Villages), and traditional culinary workshops.
* **DKU Buddy Program**: International students are paired with Korean students from Dankook University to practice conversation, build lasting friendships, and adjust to life in Korea quickly.

## Location & Campus Life
Situated in Jukjeon, Yongin, Gyeonggi-do, the campus offers a peaceful, green environment with state-of-the-art facilities. It is remarkably convenient to commute to the center of Seoul (Gangnam is reachable in under 40 minutes via the Shinbundang/Suin-Bundang Subway Lines and various express buses). The Jukjeon neighborhood boasts a vibrant student commercial area with diverse dining, trendy cafés, shopping, and convenient health services.
