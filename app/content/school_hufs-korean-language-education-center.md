## School Overview

Established in 1992, the Hankuk University of Foreign Studies (HUFS) Korean Language Education Center (KLEC) is one of South Korea's premier institutions for Korean language learning. Leveraging HUFS's global academic reputation in foreign language education, the KLEC provides highly structured and systematically designed curricula to meet the diverse academic and professional needs of international students.

With a diverse community of students from over 50 different countries, HUFS KLEC offers a uniquely international environment where students can experience global citizenship while mastering the Korean language.

## Core Strengths

* **Academic Excellence**: Backed by Korea's leading foreign language university, KLEC programs utilize state-of-the-art pedagogical methods developed by linguistic experts.
* **Balanced Language Skills**: Courses place equal emphasis on speaking, listening, reading, and writing, allowing students to communicate naturally in real-life Korean society.
* **Korean Buddy System**: KLEC connects international students with undergraduate HUFS students for language exchange, peer tutoring, and cultural adaptation support.
* **Multicultural Environment**: The ratio of students from various nationalities is well-balanced, discouraging the exclusive use of any single non-Korean language in the classroom.

## Curriculum Overview

### 1. Regular Program (10-Week Semester)
The core program is designed for academic, professional, and general purposes. It operates on a 4-quarter basis (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter).

* **Schedule**: Monday to Friday (5 days a week), 4 hours per day (Total 200 hours per semester).
* **Time Slots**: Morning (09:00 - 13:00) and Afternoon (13:10 - 17:00).
* **Levels**: Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Advanced).
* **Tuition**: Approximately 1,650,000 KRW per semester (excluding registration fee).

### 2. Short-Term Intensive Program (3-Week Course)
Ideal for students who want to improve their Korean skills and experience culture during vacations.

* **Schedule**: 3 weeks (60 hours total).
* **Tuition**: Approximately 1,050,000 KRW.
* **Content**: Combination of language learning and intensive cultural excursions.

## Campus Life & Accommodation

### Dormitories
International students have the opportunity to apply for on-campus and near-campus dormitories, such as the **GlobeeDorm** and **International House**. These facilities offer safe, affordable, and fully furnished rooms with access to laundry rooms, study halls, and high-speed internet.

### Student Facilities
Students at KLEC enjoy access to all premium university facilities, including the main library, health center, student cafeterias, fitness center, and global lounge areas.

## Admission Process

1. **Online Application Submission**: Submit application documents and pay the non-refundable application fee (approx. 60,000 KRW).
2. **Document Screening**: HUFS admissions team reviews the eligibility criteria.
3. **Tuition Payment**: Pay the invoice for tuition fees once accepted.
4. **Certificate of Admission Issued**: Receive documents required for D-4 student visa application.
5. **Visa Application & Placement Test**: Apply for the visa at the local Korean embassy and take the online/offline Korean level placement test before classes begin.
