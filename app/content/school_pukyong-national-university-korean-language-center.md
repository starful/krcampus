## School Overview

Located in the beautiful maritime city of Busan, **Pukyong National University (PKNU) Korean Language Center** is renowned for providing high-quality, systematically structured Korean language training to international students. PKNU is a highly respected national university in South Korea, offering an exceptional academic environment with one of the flatest, most pedestrian-friendly campuses in the country.

The Korean Language Center (KLC) at PKNU aims to foster global talent by combining rigorous linguistic instruction with rich cultural immersions. Whether your goal is to matriculate into a Korean undergraduate/graduate program, pass the TOPIK exam, or acquire language skills for professional development, PKNU provides a supportive and affordable pathway.

## Program Curriculum

### 1. Regular Korean Program
The Regular Program is designed for students seeking systematic learning from beginner to advanced levels (Levels 1 to 6).
* **Duration:** 10 weeks per semester (4 semesters a year: Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter)
* **Class Hours:** 200 hours per semester (4 hours a day, 5 days a week)
* **Class Size:** Approximately 15 students per class to ensure personalized attention.
* **Key Focus:** Balanced development of speaking, listening, reading, and writing, with extra emphasis on practical communication and TOPIK preparation.

### 2. Special and Short-Term Programs
During summer and winter vacations, the center runs short-term intensive camps that integrate language study with cultural excursions around Busan’s famous sites, such as Haeundae Beach, Gwangalli, and cultural villages.

## Campus Life & Accommodation

### On-Campus Housing
PKNU offers modern dormitory facilities (such as Sejong Hall) to international students. Staying on campus is highly recommended for safety, convenience, and linguistic immersion.
* **Dormitory Facilities:** Shared double rooms with private bathrooms, high-speed internet, laundry facilities, study rooms, gym, and cafeteria.
* **Cost:** Very reasonable compared to private rentals in metropolitan Seoul.

### Cultural & Support Activities
* **Korean Buddy Program:** Connects international students with domestic PKNU students to foster mutual language exchanges and cultural adjustments.
* **Field Trips:** Organized excursions every semester to historical sites, traditional markets, and scenic attractions across Busan and Gyeongsangnam-do.
* **Global Lounge:** A dedicated on-campus space where international students can relax, study, and interact with peers.

## Admissions & Requirements

### Eligibility
* Applicants must hold a high school diploma or equivalent educational background.

### Required Documents
1. Completed Application Form.
2. Copy of Passport.
3. Graduation Certificate (apostilled or consular-verified).
4. Official Academic Transcripts.
5. Bank Balance Certificate (demonstrating sufficient funds for tuition and living expenses).
6. Proof of Relationship Documents (if bank statement is under a parent's name).
