## University Overview

Established in 1939 as the nation's first engineering institute, Hanyang University has grown into one of South Korea's premier private research universities. Famously nicknamed the "Engine of Korea," Hanyang has played a pivotal role in Korea's rapid industrialization and modernization by producing top-tier engineers, entrepreneurs, and leaders.

Today, Hanyang University operates two main campuses: the Seoul Campus (located in Seongdong-gu, Seoul) and the ERICA Campus (located in Ansan). This guide focuses on the main Seoul Campus, which hosts a highly internationalized environment, cutting-edge research facilities, and world-renowned academic programs.

## Academic Excellence & Key Majors

While Hanyang is globally celebrated for its **Engineering** and **Information Technology** programs, it also boasts top-tier colleges in Business, Social Sciences, and International Studies. Some of the most popular tracks for international students include:

*   **College of Engineering:** Computer Science, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electronic Engineering.
*   **School of Business:** Accredited by AACSB, offering highly practical business education with global corporate exposure.
*   **Division of International Studies (DIS):** A fully English-taught undergraduate program focusing on international relations, global business, and East Asian studies.

## Campus Life & Location

Hanyang's Seoul campus offers a vibrant and unique setting. Uniquely, **Hanyang University Station** (Seoul Subway Line 2) is located directly inside the campus, offering unparalleled transit access to popular areas like Gangnam, Hongdae, and Myeongdong in under 20 minutes.

International students can enjoy a rich array of extracurriculars, including international student clubs, peer-to-peer mentoring (Hanyang Global Lions), and the legendary annual spring festival, **Rachios** (라치오스).

## Admissions & Scholarships

Admissions for international students are held twice a year (for Spring and Fall semesters). Applicants are evaluated based on academic performance, personal statements, and language proficiency (TOPIK for Korean-track programs, or TOEFL/IELTS for English-track programs).

### Scholarships for International Students
*   **Hanyang International Excellence Awards (HIEA):** Tuition waivers ranging from 30% to 100% based on academic merit after enrollment.
*   **TOPIK Scholarships:** Up to 100% tuition waiver for the first semester depending on the applicant's TOPIK level.

## Career & Placement

Hanyang graduates are highly sought after by major global conglomerates, including Samsung, Hyundai, SK, and LG. The university's strong alumni network and dedicated Career Development Center provide students with robust internship, mentoring, and employment opportunities both inside Korea and globally.
