## School Overview
Chung-Ang University (CAU) Language Institute, officially known as the Korean Language Education Center, is a premier institution located in Seoul, South Korea. Known for its academic rigor and vibrant global community, CAU offers a highly systematic Korean language curriculum designed for international students seeking academic, professional, or personal growth. The institute provides a perfect bridge for students aiming to transition into prestigious undergraduate or graduate programs at Chung-Ang University.

## Curriculum & Levels
The curriculum is structured across six proficiency levels (Levels 1 to 6) in accordance with the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) and the National Institute for International Education (NIIED) standards:
* **Beginner (Levels 1-2):** Focuses on basic survival Korean, daily conversations, pronunciation, and fundamental grammar.
* **Intermediate (Levels 3-4):** Emphasizes social interactions, understanding Korean media, expressing abstract opinions, and preparation for undergraduate-level academic entry.
* **Advanced (Levels 5-6):** Targets professional and academic fluency, critical thinking, literature, and specialized professional vocabulary.

## Key Program Features
* **Outstanding Faculty:** Courses are taught by professional, highly qualified educators specializing in teaching Korean as a foreign language.
* **Dormitory Placement:** International students have the opportunity to apply for CAU's state-of-the-art dormitory, Blue Mir Hall, which features excellent security, study rooms, gym facilities, and convenience stores.
* **TOPIK Preparation:** Special supplementary classes and mock exams are offered to help students achieve high scores on the Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK).
* **Cultural Activities:** The program integrates hands-on cultural experience programs, including traditional Korean ceramic-making, cooking, historic city tours, and K-Pop dance classes.

## Admission & Visa
* **Academic Requirement:** High school graduate or equivalent qualification.
* **Visa Support:** Upon acceptance and payment of tuition (usually minimum of 2 semesters required for visa issuance), CAU issues the Certificate of Admission required to apply for a D-4 (General Trainee) visa.
* **Location Advantage:** Situated right in the heart of Seoul near the Han River, CAU's Heukseok campus offers convenient transit options, allowing students to easily explore key areas like Gangnam, Hongdae, and Myeongdong.
