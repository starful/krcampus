## University Overview

Established in 1940, Sejong University is a prestigious private university located in the heart of Seoul, South Korea. Named in honor of King Sejong the Great, the creator of the Korean alphabet (Hangeul), the university is highly committed to academic excellence, innovative research, and creative thinking. Over the years, Sejong University has cemented its position as one of Asia's top-tier institutions, particularly known for its highly ranked hospitality program, advanced engineering fields, and robust software convergence departments.

With a diverse student body that includes over 3,200 international students from more than 50 countries, Sejong University provides a globalized learning environment with various programs taught entirely in English.

## Why Choose Sejong University?

* **Global Academic Rankings:** Sejong University consistently ranks within the top 300 universities globally in the QS World University Rankings. Its Hospitality and Leisure Management program is world-famous, routinely ranking in the global Top 50 and holding the #1 spot in South Korea.
* **Pioneering in Software and AI:** Selected by the Korean government as a 'Software-Centered University,' Sejong has built robust programs in artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and big data, preparing students for the forefront of the Fourth Industrial Revolution.
* **Vibrant Campus and Ideal Location:** Located in Gwangjin-gu, Seoul, the campus sits right next to the Children's Grand Park, offering a green, serene study environment while remaining highly accessible via Seoul Subway Line 7 (Children's Grand Park Station).
* **Generous Financial Support:** Sejong University offers excellent scholarship opportunities specifically designed for incoming international students, reducing the financial barrier to studying in Seoul.

## Academic Programs & Faculty Highlights

Sejong University consists of several prestigious colleges, with some of the most sought-after programs for international students being:

* **Hospitality & Tourism Management:** Globally recognized curriculum featuring deep industry partnerships, hands-on culinary/hotel practice, and internship opportunities.
* **Software Convergence & Engineering:** Top-tier facilities in nanotechnology, computer science, and robotics. 
* **Business Administration:** Fully AACSB-accredited business programs, ensuring that degrees obtained are recognized worldwide.
* **Art & Design:** Strong reputation in creative fields, including animation, fashion design, and film studies.

## International Admissions & Scholarships

Sejong University runs a streamlined admission process for international students with two main intakes per year: Spring (March) and Fall (September).

### Language Requirements
Applicants can apply using either Korean or English proficiency pathways:
* **Korean Track:** TOPIK Level 3 or higher (some engineering/art majors accept Level 2 conditionally).
* **English Track:** IELTS 5.5, TOEFL iBT 80, or equivalent official English test scores for tracks fully taught in English (such as Global Business or Hospitality programs).

### Scholarship Opportunities
* **TOPIK-based Scholarships:** Freshmen can receive up to 100% tuition waivers for their first semester based on their TOPIK level (typically TOPIK 6 yields a 100% waiver, TOPIK 5 yields 70%, and TOPIK 4 yields 50%).
* **English Proficiency Scholarships:** Freshmen applying to English-track programs can receive 30% to 80% tuition waivers based on high IELTS/TOEFL scores.
* **Academic Excellence:** Current students can maintain scholarships ranging from 20% to 70% of tuition by maintaining a high GPA (above 3.0 to 4.0 scale) in preceding semesters.

## Campus Life and Support Services

Sejong University is dedicated to making transition and integration smooth for international students:
* **On-Campus Dormitories:** The International Student Dormitory (Sae-nal Hall) offers modern, clean, and secure accommodation options right on campus.
* **Global Buddy Program:** Connects newly arrived international students with domestic Sejong students to help them adjust to life in Korea, practice the language, and explore Seoul.
* **One-Stop Service Center:** Dedicated staff members assist international students with visas, health insurance, part-time work permits, and psychological counseling.
