## University Overview
Chonnam National University (CNU), established in 1952, stands as a beacon of academic excellence and historical significance in South Korea. Located in Gwangju, the historical and cultural capital of the Honam region, CNU has played a pivotal role in the nation's democratization movement and academic modernization. Today, it hosts a vibrant international community of over 2,000 international students from more than 75 countries, offering state-of-the-art research laboratories and a sprawling, scenic green campus.

## Why Choose Chonnam National University?
* **National University Prestige**: As a flagship national institution, CNU receives significant government funding, allowing for elite-level academic infrastructure, low tuition fees, and abundant scholarship opportunities.
* **Lower Living Costs**: Living in Gwangju is significantly more affordable than living in Seoul, allowing international students to enjoy a high-quality standard of living at a fraction of the cost.
* **Strong Focus on AI & Future Tech**: With Gwangju becoming Korea's premier AI cluster hub, CNU has established the College of AI Convergence, providing direct pipelines to careers in high-tech industries.

## Comprehensive Scholarship Opportunities
CNU offers exceptional financial support to international applicants:
* **Academic Excellence Scholarships**: Newly admitted students can receive 40% to 100% tuition waivers based on their TOPIK proficiency level or English test scores (such as IELTS or TOEFL).
* **GKS (Global Korea Scholarship)**: Highly prestigious fully-funded scholarships by the Korean government are available for both undergraduate and graduate tracks.
* **On-Campus Work-Study**: Opportunities to work inside international departments or campus libraries to offset personal expenses.

## Campus Life and Student Support
Living at CNU offers a rich cultural experience. The main campus in Gwangju features expansive green parks, sports facilities, modern libraries, and multiple cafeterias serving diverse dietary options.
* **Affordable Housing**: On-campus dormitories are readily available to international students, offering safe, fully furnished options complete with high-speed internet, laundry facilities, and shared study rooms.
* **Global Student Lounge & Buddy Program**: The Office of International Affairs matches incoming students with domestic Korean buddies to facilitate linguistic and cultural adjustment.
