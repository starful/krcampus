## School Overview

Yonsei Korean Language Institute (연세대학교 한국어학당) is among the most popular Korean language programs for international students. Located in Sinchon, Seoul, it attracts learners preparing for degree programs at Korean universities.

## Programs

- **Regular intensive courses** — 200 hours per level, 4 levels per year
- **TOPIK preparation** — focused classes for levels I and II
- **Evening courses** — for residents already in Korea

## Why students choose Yonsei KLI

| Factor | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Location | Sinchon — universities, cafes, transit |
| Reputation | Long history; strong university pathway |
| Activities | Cultural classes and student events |

## Application tips

Apply early for March and September intakes. Prepare passport, diploma, and bank balance proof for visa (D-4).
