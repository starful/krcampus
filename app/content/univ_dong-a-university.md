## University Overview

Dong-A University (DAU) is a prestigious private university located in Busan, South Korea's second-largest city and primary port metropolis. Established in 1946, Dong-A University has built a legacy of academic excellence, research development, and community engagement. Under the motto of "Freedom, Truth, and Justice," the university has nurtured more than 200,000 leaders who serve in various sectors of Korean society and global industries.

Dong-A is highly regarded for its unique multi-campus structure, which distributes academic disciplines strategically across Busan to optimize industry connections and urban accessibility. For international students, Dong-A offers an ideal mix of high-quality higher education, strong career-building networks, and a vibrant, cost-effective coastal lifestyle.

---

## Why Choose Dong-A University?

### 1. The Multi-Campus Advantage
Dong-A University operates three specialized campuses in Busan, each tailored to specific academic fields:
*   **Seunghak Campus (Saha-gu):** Nestled on the slopes of Mt. Seunghak, this main campus houses the College of Engineering, Natural Sciences, Human Ecology, and Physical Education. It features state-of-the-art laboratories and extensive green spaces.
*   **Bumin Campus (Seo-gu):** Located in the heart of Busan's historic district, this modern, urban campus specializes in Business Administration, Social Sciences, and Global Studies. It integrates historically preserved heritage buildings with high-tech lecture halls.
*   **Gudeok Campus (Seo-gu):** Bordered by beautiful parks, this campus focuses on health and medical disciplines, hosting the College of Medicine, College of Nursing, and Dong-A University Hospital.

### 2. High Scholarship Opportunities for Global Students
Dong-A is exceptionally welcoming to international applicants, offering a generous tiered scholarship system based on academic entry performance (standardized test scores or TOPIK levels). Students holding a TOPIK level 5 or 6 can receive up to a 100% tuition waiver for their first semester, and continue to earn tuition waivers based on their cumulative GPA during their studies.

### 3. Lower Cost of Living and Dynamic Beach City Lifestyle
Compared to Seoul, Busan offers a much more affordable cost of living while providing top-tier urban amenities. International students enjoy world-famous beaches (Haeundae, Gwangalli), local cultural festivals, and milder winter temperatures.

## Academic Programs & Majors

Dong-A University offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate programs suitable for global students. Key faculties include:

*   **College of Engineering:** Civil, Mechanical, Chemical, Electrical, and Computer Engineering. It is well-known for producing highly employable graduates who transition into Busan's industrial belts.
*   **College of Business Administration:** Comprising Management, International Trade, and Tourism Management. The curriculum emphasizes global market insights and practical internship experiences.
*   **Global Business Academic Program:** Conducts specialized lectures in English, catering directly to international students aiming for global corporate careers.
*   **College of Design and Arts:** Highly acclaimed programs in Fashion Design, Industrial Design, and Fine Arts, utilizing creative workshop environments.

---

## Campus Life and Support Systems

Dong-A University is dedicated to making transition and integration smooth for international scholars:

*   **The Global Zone:** A designated lounge area on campus where domestic and international students mingle, practice language exchange, and attend cultural events.
*   **International Student Support Team:** A dedicated administrative office assisting with visa issues, health insurance, campus housing, and part-time job seeking permits.
*   **On-Campus Housing:** Modern dormitories equipped with cafeterias, study rooms, gym facilities, and high-speed internet are available to incoming international freshman students at competitive rates.

## Admission Guide for International Students

### Language Requirements
*   **Korean Track:** Most majors require a minimum of TOPIK Level 3 (or completing Level 3 or higher at Dong-A's Korean Language Education Center). For graduation, students typically must achieve TOPIK Level 4.
*   **English Track:** For selective global programs, English proficiency test scores (TOEFL iBT 80, IELTS 5.5, or CEFR B2 equivalent) are accepted.

### Required Documents
1.  Completed Application Form
2.  Official High School Graduation Certificate & Transcripts (with Apostille or Consular verification)
3.  Language Proficiency Certificates (TOPIK or English Test)
4.  Copy of Passport and Alien Registration Card (ARC, if applicable)
5.  Certificate of Bank Balance (typically showing $18,000 USD or equivalent to satisfy immigration requirements)
6.  Family Relationship Certificate (to verify parent-child relationship for financial sponsors)
