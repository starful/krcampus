## University Overview

Changwon National University (CWNU), established in 1969, is a prestigious national higher education institution located in Changwon, the capital city of Gyeongsangnam-do (Gyeongnam), South Korea. Known as a major hub for industrial innovation and technology, CWNU plays a pivotal role in regional development and has earned an outstanding reputation for engineering, smart manufacturing, business, and humanities.

As a national university, CWNU offers world-class education at a fraction of the cost of private universities in Seoul. The campus is beautifully integrated with nature, offering a peaceful, safe, and highly technologically advanced environment that is ideal for international students looking to succeed in Korea.

## Key Academic Strengths

CWNU is highly regarded for its specialized colleges, particularly:
- **College of Mechatronics**: One of the first and most prestigious mechatronics colleges in South Korea, deeply integrated with the local smart manufacturing industries.
- **College of Engineering**: Offering advanced tracks in mechanical, materials, chemical, and computer engineering with state-of-the-art laboratory facilities.
- **College of Gyeongsang (Business & Economics)**: Preparing global leaders with strong regional networks and comprehensive curriculum in international trade, business administration, and economics.

## Global Industry-Academia Collaboration

Situated in the heart of the Changwon National Industrial Complex, CWNU boasts unparalleled partnerships with world-renowned corporations such as Hanwha Aerospace, Hyundai Rotem, LG Electronics, and Doosan Enerbility. These collaborations translate into massive opportunities for international students, including:
- Hands-on internships and practical field training.
- Industry-sponsored research and development projects.
- Exceptional post-graduation employment pathways through specialized visa tracks (such as the F-2-R regional talent visa or E-7 professional visa).

## Low Cost of Study & Generous Scholarships

International students at CWNU enjoy highly subsidized tuition rates typical of South Korea's national universities. Additionally, the university provides extensive scholarship opportunities:
- **Admission Scholarships**: Awarded based on TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) levels or English proficiency scores (TOEFL, IELTS, etc.).
- **Academic Excellence Scholarships**: Tuition waivers ranging from 30% to 100% based on GPA performance during previous semesters.
- **Korean Language Program Pathway**: High discounts and seamless pathways for students transitioning from CWNU's Korean Language Institute to undergraduate programs.

## Campus Life and Student Support

CWNU is committed to ensuring a comfortable and enriching experience for all global students:
- **Guaranteed Dormitories**: Modern on-campus housing is widely available and very affordable, featuring cafeteria options, gym facilities, convenience stores, and study rooms.
- **Global Buddy Program**: Pairs international students with domestic Korean students to facilitate smooth cultural exchange, daily life adjustment, and language learning.
- **Dedicated International Office**: A specialized department assisting with visas, alien registration, health insurance, part-time job permits, and local adaptation programs.
