# Ultimate Study Guide: Changwon National University (CWNU)

Are you looking for an affordable, highly-ranked national university in South Korea with deep ties to global industries? Changwon National University (CWNU) is an exceptional choice. This comprehensive guide covers everything international students need to know.

---

## 1. University Overview

Changwon National University (CWNU), founded in 1969, is a premier public institution in Changwon, Gyeongsangnam-do. As a flagship national university, CWNU delivers world-class education at an affordable cost. It has a 65% acceptance rate and hosts over 680 international students.

Strategically integrated with the Changwon National Industrial Complex, the university offers robust industry-academia cooperation, giving students access to internships and careers. With high dormitory occupancy and heavily subsidized tuition, CWNU is an exceptional educational value.

---

## 2. English-Taught & International Programs

While Korean is the primary language at CWNU, the university offers English-mediated instruction (EMI) across several engineering and business courses. Programs in Mechatronics and Engineering provide a high proportion of classes in English.

For students needing Korean language training, the CWNU Korean Language Center offers an intensive Korean Language Program (KLP). It consists of four 10-week semesters annually. Completing TOPIK Level 3 or higher guarantees direct entry into undergraduate degrees.

---

CWNU operates eight diverse faculties, providing robust academic pathways. The university’s absolute strength lies in its **College of Mechatronics**, a specialized field integrating mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. This department is heavily funded by the Korean government to drive smart manufacturing and robotics innovation.

## 4. Tuition, Fees & Scholarships

CWNU offers unmatched financial benefits. As a national university, its tuition fees are heavily subsidized by the government. In an exceptional move to support students, the university has set its admission fee to 0 KRW.

| Fee Category | CWNU (National University) | Seoul Private Universities (Est.) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Admission Fee** | 0 KRW | 200,000 KRW |
| **Yearly Tuition** | 3,400,000 KRW | 8,500,000 KRW |
| **Dormitory (Per Semester)** | 1,100,000 KRW* | 2,200,000 KRW |
| **Monthly Living Expenses** | 500,000 KRW* | 850,000 KRW |

*\*Note: Figures marked with an asterisk are realistic estimates based on current student living costs.*

To further ease the financial burden, CWNU provides generous merit-based scholarships. New international students with a TOPIK Level 3 receive a 30% tuition waiver, which increases to 100% for those with TOPIK Level 5 or 6. Continuing students can maintain a 30% to 70% tuition discount by maintaining a GPA of 3.0 or higher.

---

## 5. Admissions for International Students

Admissions at CWNU are held for the Spring (March) and Fall (September) semesters.

### Key Requirements
1. **Academic:** Applicants and parents must be non-Korean, and the applicant must have completed high school.
2. **Language:** TOPIK Level 3 for Korean tracks; TOEFL iBT 80 or IELTS 5.5 for English tracks.
3. **Financial:** A bank balance showing at least $16,000 USD.

The process involves online document submission and an online faculty interview.

---

## 6. Campus Life & Location

Located at 20 Changwondae-hakro, Uichang-gu, Changwon, CWNU boasts a beautiful, flat campus that is highly walkable. Changwon is a planned eco-city, offering high safety, clean air, and extensive green spaces.

One of CWNU's major benefits is its **high dormitory occupancy rate**. Almost all international applicants secure a spot in the modern on-campus dormitories. The residential halls feature gym facilities, study rooms, high-speed internet, and international kitchens.

Moreover, Changwon is a global industrial hub. It hosts manufacturing headquarters for major corporations such as LG, Hanwha, and Hyundai Rotem. This gives students prime access to site visits, industrial mentorship, and high-paying jobs in smart manufacturing and tech industries, all while being just 30 minutes away from the metropolitan buzz of Busan.

## 7. FAQ

### What is the acceptance rate for international students?
The international student acceptance rate is around 65%. Students who meet the academic requirements and hold the necessary language certifications stand a very strong chance of admission.

### Can I work part-time while studying at CWNU?
Yes. International students on a D-2 visa can work up to 20 hours per week during the semester and unlimited hours during vacations. You must obtain permission from the university and the local immigration office.

### Is Korean proficiency required for admission?
No, it is not mandatory if you apply for the English-taught programs in the College of Mechatronics or Engineering. However, basic Korean will greatly help with daily life and part-time jobs.

### How guaranteed is on-campus housing?
With an exceptionally high dormitory occupancy rate, housing is guaranteed for almost all incoming international students during their first year, provided they apply during the standard registration period.

### What career support does the university offer?
CWNU's Career Support Center provides targeted assistance for international students, including resume building, interview simulations, and local job fairs connecting students directly with Changwon's massive industrial sector.
