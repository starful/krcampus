## School Overview
Kangnam University Korean Language Center, located in the dynamic city of Yongin, Gyeonggi Province, offers high-quality Korean language training designed for international students aiming to pursue higher education, employment, or personal interest in Korea.

Since its establishment, the center has provided systematic education, combining grammar, speaking, listening, and writing. With a student-oriented curriculum, experienced faculty, and supportive campus services, it serves as an excellent gateway to academic and cultural life in South Korea.

## Key Program Features
- **Structured 6-Level Curriculum**: From beginner (Level 1) to advanced (Level 6) levels, aligning with TOPIK standards.
- **Pathway to Degree Programs**: Students completing the upper levels of the language program are eligible for special admissions and scholarships for undergraduate or graduate degrees at Kangnam University.
- **Korean Culture Experiences**: Culturally rich activities, such as Hanbok experiences, historic site field trips, and K-Pop/K-Food activities are organized each semester.
- **Student Support**: Global student coordinators and buddy programs pair international students with local Korean peers to ease transition and enhance language practice.

## Accommodation & Dormitory
Kangnam University provides comfortable, modern on-campus housing (Simjeon Hall) for international language students. The dormitories are equipped with double-occupancy rooms, study areas, laundry facilities, high-speed internet, and shared dining options, ensuring a safe and convenient learning environment.

## Campus Location & Transportation
The campus is conveniently located in Yongin, Gyeonggi Province, easily accessible via the Everline Line (Kangnam University Station). It offers the perfect mix of a quiet, scholarly environment with easy, direct transit options to the Seoul metropolitan area, Gangnam, and major regional spots like Everland and the Korean Folk Village.
