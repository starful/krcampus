## School Overview

Established in 1971, the **Konkuk University Language Education Institute (KU LEI)** is one of the premier Korean language training institutions in Seoul. Located on Konkuk University's main campus in Gwangjin-gu, the institute offers a vibrant, immersive academic environment surrounded by a beautiful green campus featuring the famous Ilgam Lake.

With a highly experienced faculty and a state-of-the-art curriculum, KU LEI serves thousands of international students annually, helping them master the Korean language and transition smoothly into Korean higher education or professional careers.

## Curriculum & Class Structure

KU LEI's regular program consists of four terms per year (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter), with each term running for 10 weeks (totaling 200 class hours).

* **Levels Offered:** Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Highly Advanced).
* **Class Schedule:** Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM (Morning session) or 1:30 PM - 5:30 PM (Afternoon session).
* **Class Size:** Averaging 12-15 students per class to ensure personalized attention and active speaking practice.
* **Core Focus:** Balanced integration of Speaking, Listening, Reading, and Writing, alongside specialized preparation for the TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) exam.

## Student Benefits & Campus Life

* **KU:L House Dormitory:** International students have access to Konkuk's premium on-campus residential complex, featuring modern facilities, fitness centers, study rooms, and multi-layered security.
* **Language Exchange Program:** Pair up with native Korean students from Konkuk University to practice conversational Korean and adapt to daily life in Seoul.
* **Cultural Excursions:** Every semester, the school hosts cultural activities such as making traditional food, wearing Hanbok, visiting historic palaces, and attending contemporary cultural shows.
* **Academic Pathways:** High-performing language students receive admission counseling and tuition discounts (scholarships) when applying for undergraduate or graduate degrees at Konkuk University.
