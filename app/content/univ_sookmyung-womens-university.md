## University Overview
Sookmyung Women's University is a prestigious private university located in the heart of Seoul, South Korea. Established in 1906 by the royal family of the Joseon Dynasty, it holds the historic distinction of being Korea's very first royal private educational institution for women. Over more than a century, Sookmyung has cultivated generations of female leaders, shaping the educational, social, and political landscape of modern Korea. 

With its motto "Gentle Power, Changing the World," Sookmyung focuses on empowering women through comprehensive academic disciplines, global engagement, and values-based leadership training. The campus blends beautiful historic buildings with modern, state-of-the-art research facilities, providing an ideal academic environment for domestic and international students alike.

## Academic Strengths
Sookmyung offers a wide array of programs across liberal arts, natural sciences, engineering, and the arts. 

* **School of Global Service:** This highly acclaimed division offers specialized tracks in Global Cooperation and Entrepreneurship. Many courses are taught entirely in English, attracting a large concentration of international scholars.
* **College of Engineering:** Successfully expanded and modernized in recent years, this faculty offers competitive programs in Computer Science, Software, and Chemical and Biological Engineering, breaking boundaries for women in STEM.
* **Business & Economics:** Fully accredited and highly recognized, this college prepares students for leadership positions in multinational corporations with an emphasis on ethics, strategy, and analytical skills.
* **Korean Language and Culture:** Sookmyung’s Korean Language Institute (Lingua Express) is highly regarded for its structured curricula, helping foreign students master the language rapidly.

## Campus Life & Location
Sookmyung is situated in Cheongpadong, Yongsan-gu—the absolute geographical center of Seoul. 
* **Superb Accessibility:** The campus is just minutes away from Seoul Station and Namyeong Station, making travel anywhere inside or outside the capital exceptionally easy. 
* **Vibrant Neighborhood:** The university neighborhood features a charming alleyway culture lined with cozy cafes, global restaurants, and boutiques tailored to student budgets. Popular districts like Itaewon, Yongsan, and Myeongdong are only a short subway ride away.
* **Facilities:** Students have access to the massive Samsung Convention Center, the Central Library equipped with private study pods, high-tech computer labs, and the historic Sookmyung Women's University Museum.

## Support for International Students
Sookmyung provides a warm, welcoming, and safe environment for international scholars through diverse support systems:
* **Sookmyung Peer Group (URINK):** A dedicated student-led mentoring program that matches international students with local Sookmyungians to assist with registration, campus navigation, and adapting to daily life in Korea.
* **Global Lounge:** A designated multicultural space on campus designed for language exchange, international events, and global networking.
* **Scholarships:** Generous tuition waivers ranging from 30% to 100% are awarded to incoming and enrolled international students based on academic performance and Korean/English language proficiency test scores.
