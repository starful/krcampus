## School Overview

The SNU Language Education Institute offers structured Korean programs on the main Gwanak campus — ideal for students targeting academic Korean and future degree admission.

## Highlights

- Rigorous curriculum aligned with university-level Korean
- Library and campus facilities access
- Strong peer network of motivated learners

## Costs (approximate)

Tuition varies by term length; budget **₩1.5–2.0M per 10-week term** plus housing in Gwanak or nearby.

## Visa

Most students enter on **D-4 (language training)** before transitioning to D-2 for degree programs.
