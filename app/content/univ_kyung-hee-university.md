## University Overview

Established in 1949, **Kyung Hee University (KHU)** is a premier private research university in South Korea, highly recognized for its academic excellence, global outlook, and commitment to creating a "peaceful society." Spanning across two main campuses—the Seoul Campus (focusing on humanities, social sciences, and medicine) and the Global Campus in Suwon (focusing on applied sciences, engineering, and modern arts)—Kyung Hee offers a comprehensive educational ecosystem.

KHU is particularly renowned for its breathtaking campus aesthetics. The Gothic-style Peace Hall and majestic European-inspired architecture draw thousands of visitors during spring cherry blossom season, making it one of the most inspiring learning environments in Asia.

---

## Academic Excellence & World-Class Faculties

Kyung Hee University consistently ranks within the top 300 universities worldwide (QS World University Rankings) and is highly competitive globally in several specific disciplines:

1. **Hotel, Tourism & Food Service Management**: KHU's College of Hotel and Tourism Management is widely regarded as the absolute best in South Korea and ranks in the top 10 globally. It has produced generations of industry leaders, CEOs, and global hospitality experts.
2. **Korean Traditional Medicine**: Kyung Hee is the undisputed pioneer and national leader in traditional Korean medicine (Hanui-hak), fusing Eastern healing practices with modern biomedical sciences.
3. **Global/International Studies & Liberal Arts**: Through the Humanitas College, Kyung Hee emphasizes a holistic liberal arts curriculum, ensuring all students graduate with strong critical thinking, ethics, and global citizenship skills.

## Admissions Guide for International Students

Kyung Hee welcomes a highly diverse student body, boasting one of the largest international student enrollments in South Korea (over 4,800 students from more than 80 countries).

### Language Requirements
* **Korean Track**: Minimum TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) Level 3 is required for application, though Level 4 or higher is highly recommended to easily cope with academic courses. Some specific majors might demand higher proficiency.
* **English Track**: For programs taught completely in English, proof of English proficiency is required (typically TOEFL iBT 80, IELTS 5.5, or higher).

### Required Documents
* Completed Application Form
* High School Graduation Certificate (Apostilled or Consular Verified)
* Academic Transcripts
* Proof of Language Proficiency (TOPIK or English Test Results)
* Copy of Passport (Applicant and Parents)
* Financial Verification (Bank balance certificate demonstrating at least $20,000 USD or equivalent)

## Tuition, Fees, and Scholarships

While tuition rates vary by department, the average estimated fees are as follows:

* **Admission Fee**: Approximately 150,000 KRW (One-time, non-refundable)
* **Average Undergraduate Tuition (Per Semester)**:
  * Humanities & Social Sciences: ~3,800,000 to 4,200,000 KRW
  * Natural Sciences & Physical Education: ~4,500,000 to 4,800,000 KRW
  * Engineering, Arts, & Hospitality: ~5,000,000 to 5,500,000 KRW

### International Scholarship Opportunities
Kyung Hee is generous with merit-based financial aid for newcomers and enrolled students:
* **Admission Scholarships**: Based on entrance evaluation scores and TOPIK levels. A TOPIK Level 6 can secure up to a 100% tuition waiver for the first semester.
* **Academic Excellence Scholarships**: Awarded to current students who maintain top-tier GPAs, offering 30% to 100% tuition coverage for subsequent semesters.

## Campus Life & Student Support

Kyung Hee ensures international students adapt smoothly to Korean life through dedicated administrative bodies and peer programs:

* **Global Service Center (GSC)**: The central hub for all international student affairs, handling visas, health insurance, orientation, and general welfare.
* **KHU Buddy Program**: Pairs incoming international freshmen with domestic Korean students to foster intercultural friendships, assist with language learning, and help navigate the Seoul campus.
* **Housing**: On-campus dormitories (such as Sameuiwon and Sewha Hall in Seoul) are available, providing clean, secure, and affordable accommodation options equipped with high-speed internet, study rooms, and gym facilities.
