# Soongsil University: The Ultimate International Student Guide

For students seeking heritage, technology, and a vibrant Seoul lifestyle, **Soongsil University (숭실대학교)** is an exceptional choice. Founded in 1897 as Korea's first modern university, Soongsil is a global leader in Computer Science, AI, and IT Convergence, offering international students a supportive academic environment.

---

## 1. University Overview

*   **Location:** 369 Sangdo-ro, Dongjak-gu, Seoul, 06978, Republic of Korea
*   **First Modern University:** Founded in Pyongyang in 1897, Soongsil has a rich heritage, playing a pivotal role in Korea's educational development.
*   **National IT Leader:** Established Korea's first Computer Science department and continues to lead in AI and IT convergence.
*   **Subway Access:** Soongsil University Station (Line 7) is directly at the main gate, offering easy transit access across Seoul.
*   **Support & Scholarships:** Comprehensive academic, language, and financial assistance tailored for international students.

Soongsil hosts approximately **2,150 international students**. With an estimated acceptance rate of **35% - 45%**, the admissions process is competitive yet accessible.

---

## 2. English-Taught & International Programs

Soongsil provides several pathways for English-speaking and international students:

*   **Global Commerce & Business:** The College of Economics & Global Commerce offers English-friendly lectures in international trade and business management.
*   **General Education:** Baird University College coordinates diverse English-medium liberal arts and social science electives.
*   **Korean Language Institute (KLI):** Offers intensive quarterly language training (four semesters per year) for students looking to meet TOPIK requirements.
*   **Exchange Programs:** Partnerships with over 400 global universities allow students to study at Soongsil for 1-2 semesters with English-taught coursework.

---

## 3. Faculties & Academic Strengths

Soongsil comprises nine colleges offering specialized academic tracks:

*   **College of IT:** A top-tier department covering Computer Science, Global Media, Software, and AI Convergence.
*   **College of Engineering:** Programs in Chemical, Mechanical, and Organic Materials engineering.
*   **Economics & Global Commerce:** Home to the popular Department of Global Commerce for international business leaders.
*   **Business Administration:** Focuses on business management, finance, and entrepreneurship.
*   **College of Law:** Known for producing highly competent legal and public administration professionals.
*   **Social Sciences:** Explores sociology, social welfare, journalism, and political science.
*   **Humanities:** Focuses on literature, history, philosophy, and various language departments.
*   **Natural Sciences:** Offers programs in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Bioinformatics.
*   **Baird University College:** The central hub for foundational liberal arts and global leadership.

## 6. Campus Life & Location

The campus is located in Dongjak-gu, a safe, central residential district in Seoul.

*   **Subway Access:** The campus features its own stop (**Soongsil University Station, Line 7**), placing Gangnam just 15 minutes away.
*   **Dormitories:** On-campus housing is guaranteed for most incoming freshmen. Rooms are fully furnished, featuring air conditioning and Wi-Fi.
*   **Global Buddy Program:** Pairs international students with Korean peers to assist with campus adjustment, course registration, and language practice.

## 7. FAQ

### Can I apply to Soongsil University without knowing any Korean?
Yes. You can apply to English-taught programs or enroll in the **Korean Language Institute (KLI)** to achieve the necessary TOPIK level before starting your degree.

### What is the estimated cost of living for a student at Soongsil?
Monthly living costs average **900,000 to 1,300,000 KRW**. This covers dorms (350,000–450,000 KRW), meals, transit, and personal expenses.

### Are international students allowed to work part-time in Korea?
Yes. D-2 visa holders can work up to 20 hours per week during semesters (unlimited during breaks) with university and immigration approval. TOPIK proficiency opens more employment options.

### What makes Soongsil’s IT and Computer Science departments unique?
Soongsil founded Korea's first Computer Science department in 1970. It remains a national leader in IT, AI, and software, offering excellent industry connections and research facilities.

### Does the university offer airport pickup services for new international students?
Yes. The Office of International Affairs and the Global Buddy network offer airport pickup and orientation programs at the start of each semester.
