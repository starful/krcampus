## University Overview

Jeonbuk National University (JBNU), founded in 1947, is one of the ten flagship Korean National Universities. Located in the historic city of Jeonju, JBNU has grown into a major hub for global research and high-quality higher education. The university spans a beautiful, green campus featuring a unique blend of modern facilities and traditional Korean Hanok architecture.

As a government-supported flagship national university, JBNU is dedicated to offering affordable, world-class education. It routinely ranks among the top 10 national universities in South Korea and has established partnerships with hundreds of universities worldwide, attracting over 1,600 international students from more than 70 countries.


## Why Choose Jeonbuk National University?

* **Exceptional Affordability:** Tuition fees at JBNU are heavily subsidized by the South Korean government. International students pay a fraction of what private universities in Seoul charge, making it one of the most cost-effective pathways to a prestigious degree in Korea.
* **Highly Decorated Research & Academics:** JBNU is renowned for its strength in engineering, agricultural sciences, and veterinary medicine. It boasts state-of-the-art laboratory facilities and receives massive government funding for research and development.
* **Jeonju: The Cultural Heart of Korea:** Studying at JBNU allows students to experience authentic Korean heritage. Jeonju is a designated UNESCO City of Gastronomy and home to the world-famous Jeonju Hanok Village. The cost of living here is also significantly lower than in the Seoul metropolitan area.
* **Strong Support Systems:** From the 'Oasis' portal system to dedicated international student advisors, free Korean language tutoring, and cultural excursion programs, JBNU ensures overseas students integrate seamlessly into local life.


## Academic Offerings & Top Faculties

JBNU offers a massive range of undergraduate, master's, and doctoral programs across its diverse colleges. Key areas of study highly recommended for international students include:

* **Engineering & IT:** With direct connections to national industrial complexes, JBNU's engineering programs (Mechanical, Chemical, and Smart Semiconductor Engineering) offer superb job placement opportunities.
* **Agricultural & Life Sciences:** Situated near Korea's major agricultural research hubs, this college is internationally recognized for innovative research in biotechnology and food science.
* **College of Commerce (Business Administration):** Offers globally focused business and trade curriculums designed to groom international commerce experts.
* **Korean Language and Literature:** A fantastic department for students looking to specialize in Korean language pedagogy, translation, or cultural studies.


## Tuition & Generous Scholarships

As a national university, JBNU boasts incredibly competitive tuition rates. 

* **Undergraduate Tuition:** Approximately 1,700,000 KRW to 2,500,000 KRW per semester (varying by humanities, sciences, or engineering majors).
* **Admission Fee:** Following national education reforms, JBNU has eliminated undergraduate admission fees.

### Scholarship Opportunities
JBNU offers extensive financial support packages for global students:
* **Freshmen Scholarship:** Awarded based on incoming TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) scores or English proficiency certificates (IELTS/TOEFL). Students with TOPIK Level 5 or 6 often receive a 100% tuition waiver for their first semester.
* **Academic Excellence Scholarship:** Active students who maintain a high GPA (above 3.0 to 4.0 scale) can receive partial to full tuition waivers for subsequent semesters.
* **Global Korea Scholarship (GKS):** JBNU is a designated partner institution for this fully funded government scholarship program, which covers full tuition, living expenses, airfare, and Korean language training.


## Campus Life & Accommodation

JBNU offers some of the best campus residential facilities in the country. The on-campus dormitories (such as Daedong Hall and Woongbi Hall) provide safe, clean, and highly affordable housing options for international students. Most rooms are double-occupancy, equipped with high-speed internet, private bathrooms, desk spaces, and climate control.

Beyond academics, the university boasts an active student union, multiple sports facilities (including an indoor swimming pool and gym), and dozens of student clubs ranging from traditional music to modern dance, soccer, and global exchange groups.
