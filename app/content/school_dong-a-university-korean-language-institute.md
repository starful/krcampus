## School Overview
Dong-A University, established in 1946, is a prestigious private university located in Busan, South Korea's second-largest city. The Dong-A University Korean Language Institute (KLI) offers high-quality, systematic Korean language programs specifically designed for international students, diaspora Koreans, and foreign residents who wish to master the Korean language for academic, professional, or personal pursuits.

Classes are primarily held at the Bumin Campus, situated in the heart of Busan, providing students with access to state-of-the-art facilities, historical landmarks, and a vibrant urban life.

## Curriculum
### Regular Program
The Regular Program is a comprehensive academic course designed to improve students' overall Korean communication skills across four main disciplines: speaking, listening, reading, and writing.
- **Duration**: 10 weeks per semester (200 hours total per term, 4 terms per year: Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter)
- **Schedule**: Monday to Friday (4 hours per day, either morning or afternoon sessions)
- **Levels**: Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Advanced)
- **Class Size**: Average of 15 students per class to ensure personalized feedback and interactive learning.

### Special Classes
- **TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) Preparation**: Targeted elective classes for students planning to enter undergraduate or graduate programs.
- **Korean Culture Classes**: Integrated sessions on traditional Korean calligraphy, pottery, cooking, Taekwondo, and field trips around Busan.

## Accommodation
Dong-A University provides comfortable and modern housing options for international students:
- **Hanlim Dormitory**: On-campus residential halls equipped with high-speed internet, study rooms, gym facilities, laundry rooms, and lounges.
- **Room Options**: Double-occupancy rooms with private bathrooms and individual desks.
- **Support**: 24/7 security services and dormitory managers who assist international students in adjusting to living in South Korea.

## Student Support & Benefits
- **Mentoring Program**: International students are paired with Korean student buddies to practice conversational Korean and adapt to campus life.
- **D-2 Visa Support**: Dedicated staff members help with visa issuance, alien registration, and part-time work permit applications.
- **Scholarships**: Tuition waivers and academic excellence awards are given to top-performing students each term.
