## University Overview
Dongguk University, established in 1906, is a prestigious private coeducational university in South Korea. Rooted in the Buddhist philosophy of cultivating wisdom and compassion, the university welcomes students of all backgrounds and faiths. It is highly regarded for its central location, world-class alumni in the entertainment and public service sectors, and robust support systems for international students.

## Unique Identity & Heritage
As one of the few Buddhist-affiliated universities in the world, Dongguk blends historical tradition with modern innovation. The campus features beautiful, peaceful architectural elements, including temple-like structures and statues, offering a serene atmosphere in the heart of bustling Seoul. Despite its religious foundation, there is no pressure on students to practice Buddhism, and the curriculum focuses on fostering global leadership and critical thinking.

## Academic Strengths & Key Faculties
Dongguk University is highly competitive across several key fields:
* **Department of Theater and Film:** Widely recognized as one of the best in South Korea, having produced legendary actors, directors, and K-pop idols (such as Jun Ji-hyun, Lee Seung-gi, and SNSD's Yoona).
* **Police Administration:** The first and most prestigious program of its kind in South Korea, producing many of the nation's high-ranking police officials.
* **Business School & AI Convergence:** Dongguk’s Business School holds prestigious AACSB accreditation, and its new AI Convergence division prepares students for the future of tech.

## Campus Life and Location
Situated right next to **Namsan Mountain** in the center of Seoul, Dongguk offers stunning views of the Namsan Seoul Tower and easy access to major cultural landmarks. 
* **Proximity to Key Districts:** The campus is walking distance or a short subway ride from Myeongdong (the famous shopping district), Euljiro, and Dongdaemun.
* **Global Student Lounge:** A dedicated hub for international students to study, socialize, and participate in cultural exchange programs.
* **Festivals:** The annual spring festival at Dongguk is famous for its lively atmosphere, star-studded performances, and the spectacular Lotus Lantern Festival parade celebrating Buddha's birthday.

## Support and Scholarships for International Students
Dongguk University is highly proactive in recruiting and supporting global talent:
* **Admission Scholarships:** Newly admitted international students can receive a 30% to 50% tuition waiver based on their TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) level or English proficiency test scores.
* **Academic Scholarships:** Active students can maintain up to a 100% tuition waiver by maintaining high GPAs in subsequent semesters.
* **Korean Language Program:** The Dongguk Korean Language Institute (KLI) is globally renowned for its intensive immersion programs, preparing students for undergraduate study.
