## School Overview
Gangnam Korean Language Academy (GKLA) is a premier educational institution located in the dynamic heart of Gangnam, Seoul. Recognized for its highly interactive teaching methodologies and student-centric curriculum, the academy serves a diverse student body from over 40 countries. Whether you are aiming to enroll in a top-tier Korean university, seeking employment in Korea, or simply passionate about K-culture, GKLA offers customized pathways to help you achieve fluency quickly and effectively.

## Why Choose Gangnam Korean Language Academy?
- **The Ultimate Location**: Situated just steps away from Gangnam Station (Line 2 and Shinbundang Line), the academy offers students unparalleled access to Seoul's trendiest cafes, shops, libraries, and transportation links.
- **Small-Group Learning Environment**: With a strict cap of 12 students per class, instructors can provide individual feedback, significantly boosting speaking confidence and interactive communication skills.
- **Experienced Faculty**: All instructors hold certified degrees in Teaching Korean as a Foreign Language and bring years of experience working with diverse global student demographics.
- **Modern Facilities**: Equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, modern study lounges, media rooms, and self-study zones to guarantee an optimal learning environment.

## Academic Offerings

### 1. Regular Intensive Program
Our flagship course designed for comprehensive academic mastery. It covers speaking, listening, reading, and writing over a 10-week semester. Excellent for university preparation, the course aligns directly with TOPIK levels 1 through 6.

### 2. Short-Term Speaking Intensive
A highly practical, communication-first curriculum designed for travelers, expats, and digital nomads who want to prioritize conversational survival Korean and native-like slang.

### 3. TOPIK Preparation Course
A focused, strategy-driven program utilizing past exam analysis, mock tests, and specialized writing workshops to ensure students maximize their scores on the Test of Proficiency in Korean.

## Cultural Experience Program
At GKLA, language learning extends far beyond the classroom. Every semester, students can participate in curated cultural workshops including:
- K-Pop dance classes led by professional backup dancers
- Traditional Hanbok styling and tea ceremonies
- Guided street-food tours around historic districts of Seoul
- Language exchange meetups with Korean university students
