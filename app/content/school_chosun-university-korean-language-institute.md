## School Overview
Chosun University Language Education Institute, located in the culturally rich city of Gwangju, South Korea, is dedicated to providing high-quality Korean language education to international students. Established with a vision to foster global talent, the institute offers a supportive learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty.

## Curriculum & Programs
### Regular Program
The core offering is the Regular Korean Language Program, which operates on a 4-semester system (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter). Each semester runs for 10 weeks, providing 200 hours of intensive language instruction.
* **Levels:** Beginner (Level 1-2) to Advanced (Level 5-6)
* **Class Schedule:** Monday to Friday, 4 hours per day (usually 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
* **Focus Areas:** Speaking, listening, reading, writing, and practical grammar.

### Special Classes
* **TOPIK Preparation:** Special preparation classes designed to help students achieve high scores in the Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK).
* **Cultural Activities:** Once or twice a semester, students participate in field trips, traditional Korean craft workshops, and food experiences to better understand Korean culture.

## Campus Life & Accommodation
### Housing
Chosun University offers excellent on-campus dormitories for international students. New students are prioritized for housing, ensuring a safe and comfortable transition to life in Korea. Rooms are fully equipped with basic furniture, internet access, and shared or private bathroom options.

### Campus Facilities
Students at the Korean Language Institute have full access to university amenities, including:
* The Central Library
* Fitness center and sports facilities
* On-campus medical clinic
* Student cafeterias and convenience stores

## Admission & Visa Information
To apply for the regular language program, international applicants typically need:
1. Completed application form
2. High school graduation certificate (or higher diploma)
3. Proof of financial self-sufficiency (bank balance statement)
4. Passport copy and photos

Successful applicants are eligible to apply for a D-4-1 (General Student) visa.
