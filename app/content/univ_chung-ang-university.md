## University Overview
Chung-Ang University (CAU) is a premier private institution founded in 1918. Highly regarded for its outstanding academic standards and vibrant campus atmosphere, CAU is particularly famous for being the absolute pioneer in the fields of Media, Communication, Theater, and Film in South Korea. The university's mascot is the Blue Dragon, symbolizing prosperity and prestige.

## Why International Students Choose CAU
* **Global Hub of Culture and Media**: Many of Korea's top actors, directors, and broadcasters are CAU alumni. It is the go-to university for students seeking to enter the entertainment, media, and creative arts sectors.
* **World-Class Business Programs**: The College of Business and Economics is AACSB-accredited and hosts one of the largest and most dynamic undergraduate bodies in South Korea.
* **Robust Support Systems**: CAU offers an exemplary system for international student care, including the 'CAU Global Ambassador' peer-mentoring program, specialized career consulting, and dedicated academic counseling.

## Campus Locations
1. **Seoul Campus (Heukseok)**: Located in Dongjak-gu, right along the scenic Han River. It is exceptionally well-connected via Subway Line 9 (Heukseok Station), making commuting to business centers like Gangnam and Yeouido incredibly convenient.
2. **Da Vinci Campus (Anseong)**: Dedicated heavily to Arts, Sports, and Biotechnology, offering vast facilities and state-of-the-art laboratory spaces.

## Admissions & Requirements
International applicants can apply through special foreign admission tracks. 
* **Language Requirements (Korean Track)**: TOPIK Level 3 or higher is typically required for admission, but graduating requires achieving TOPIK Level 4.
* **Language Requirements (English Track)**: English proficiency tests like TOEFL iBT 80 or IELTS 5.5 (or higher) are mandatory for English-taught majors (e.g., Global Business).

## Tuition & Scholarships
While tuition fees vary by major (humanities being lower, engineering and art being higher), CAU offers competitive pricing and extensive scholarship options:
* **CAU Global Scholarship**: Based on TOPIK scores, incoming freshmen can receive a 30% to 100% waiver on their tuition fee for the first semester. Academic excellence scholarships are awarded in subsequent semesters based on GPA.
