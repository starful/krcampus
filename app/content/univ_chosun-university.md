## University Overview

Chosun University, located in the culturally rich city of Gwangju, is one of South Korea's most historic and respected private higher education institutions. Founded in 1946 by a citizen-led association of 72,000 members, the university carries a unique legacy of being built by the community, for the community. This spirit of public service, combined with academic excellence, has propelled Chosun University into a major regional hub for research, global business, and cultural preservation.

With over 20,000 enrolled students across dozens of undergraduate and graduate departments, Chosun boasts a vibrant, self-contained campus. Its iconic, pristine white Main Building is a recognized architectural landmark visible from almost anywhere in Gwangju. For international students, Chosun offers a fantastic balance of high-quality Korean higher education, practical career pathways, and an affordable, culturally immersive lifestyle outside the congested metropolitan area of Seoul.

## Why Choose Chosun University?

### 1. Affordable Living and Study Expenses
Studying in Seoul can be incredibly expensive in terms of both tuition fees and daily living costs. Gwangju, known as Korea's "City of Light," offers a much more accessible cost of living. Rent, food, and entertainment in Gwangju are roughly 30% to 40% cheaper than in Seoul, making Chosun University an ideal choice for budget-conscious international students.

### 2. Generous Scholarships for International Students
Chosun University is highly proactive in recruiting international talent. They offer a sliding scale of tuition waivers based on your TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) score or English proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL). Outstanding students can easily secure 30% to 100% tuition waivers for their initial semesters, with continuous support based on maintaining their GPA.

### 3. Renowned Campus and Vibrant Culture
The campus is widely celebrated for its aesthetics. Chosun University hosts a famous annual **Rose Festival** in May, attracting thousands of visitors to its lush rose gardens. The university is deeply integrated into Gwangju’s identity as the birthplace of modern Korean democracy and a capital of Korean art and gastronomy.

### 4. Strong Focus on Practical Engineering and ICT
Chosun has deep ties with regional industries in Gwangju, particularly in the automotive, energy, optical, and smart-manufacturing sectors. The College of Engineering and the College of ICT/Electronics are highly regarded, providing students with excellent lab facilities, research opportunities, and local internship networks.

## Academic Offerings

Chosun University offers a comprehensive range of programs. While the primary language of instruction is Korean, select graduate and global business programs are tailored with English-track coursework.

*   **College of Global Business & Convergence:** Designed to foster global leaders through courses in international trade, business management, and convergence technologies.
*   **College of Foreign Studies:** Perfect for students wishing to specialize in Asian and Western languages, culture, and regional studies.
*   **College of Engineering & ICT:** Highly esteemed programs in Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, Computer, and Electronics Engineering, heavily aligned with Korea's advanced tech sectors.
*   **College of Art and Design:** Cultivates creative talent in Fine Arts, Visual Communication Design, and Fashion Design, drawing inspiration from Gwangju's famous art Gwangju Biennale.

## Campus Life & Accommodation

### On-Campus Housing
Chosun University provides excellent on-campus dormitories specifically optimized for international students. The **Green Village** and **Global House** dorms offer modern amenities including:
*   High-speed Wi-Fi and air-conditioned rooms (typically double-occupancy).
*   In-building convenience stores, gyms, study rooms, and laundry facilities.
*   Affordable dining plans offering local Korean cuisine.

### The Gwangju Lifestyle
Gwangju is a warm, hospitable city of 1.4 million residents. It is renowned for having the best food in Korea ("Namdo food") and is highly accessible via high-speed KTX trains, linking you to Seoul in just under two hours. The city's rich history, surrounding mountains (like Mudeungsan National Park), and bustling student districts adjacent to the campus ensure that your weekends will always be filled with adventure.

## Step-by-Step Admission Strategy for International Students

1.  **Language Proficiency First:** To enter directly into regular undergraduate programs, a minimum of **TOPIK Level 3** (sometimes Level 4 for specific majors) is highly recommended. If you do not have this, apply first to **Chosun’s Korean Language Institute** for a 6-month to 1-year intensive program.
2.  **Prepare Financial Documents:** Korean immigration requires international applicants to show a bank balance of approximately $16,000 USD to $20,000 USD to secure a D-2 student visa.
3.  **Submit Early for Scholarships:** To secure the best dorm placement and automatic merit-based tuition waivers, ensure you submit your online application and hard-copy documents at least 4 months before the semester starts (deadlines are typically in October for the Spring semester and April for the Fall semester).
