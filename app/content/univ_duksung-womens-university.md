## University Overview

Founded in 1920 by Cha Mirisa, a pioneering female independent activist, **Duksung Women's University** holds a proud history of championing women's education and self-reliance in South Korea. Located in the scenic northern district of Dobong-gu, Seoul, Duksung offers a peaceful, green academic environment contrasted with easy access to the vibrant metro center of Seoul.

Duksung has evolved into a modern, forward-thinking university that emphasizes creative thinking, global competency, and interdisciplinary study. It is highly regarded for its student-centered education systems and safe, supportive campus environment.

---

## Academic Excellence & The Free-Major System

One of Duksung's most prominent features is its highly flexible academic system. 

* **The Free-Major Entry (Global Convergence College):** International students entering the Global Convergence sector can enroll without declaring a specific major. During their first year, they explore various disciplines spanning Humanities, Social Sciences, and Business Administration before selecting their official major in their sophomore year.
* **Technology & Art:** Duksung also boasts strong programs in Science & Technology (including Computer Science and Cyber Security) and Art & Design, which is highly competitive and well-respected within Seoul.

---

## Admissions and Scholarship Schemes

To attract top international talent, Duksung Women's University offers structured financial support based on language proficiency:

* **Admission Requirements:** Applicants must submit evidence of high school graduation. Language requirements typically demand a minimum of **TOPIK Level 3** (or completion of Level 3 or higher at a certified university Korean language institute).
* **Tuition Waivers:** 
  * **TOPIK Level 3:** Typically eligible for a 30% to 40% tuition fee waiver for the first semester.
  * **TOPIK Level 4:** 40% to 50% tuition reduction.
  * **TOPIK Level 5 or 6:** Up to 100% tuition waiver for the first semester.
  * **Academic Scholarships:** Consistent GPA maintenance during undergraduate study can secure partial to full scholarships for subsequent semesters.

---

## Campus Life and Accommodation

Duksung's campus is famous for its natural beauty. Sitting right next to Bukhansan National Park, students enjoy fresh air, stunning cherry blossoms in spring, and vibrant foliage in autumn. The traditional Hanok-style guest house on campus adds a distinct Korean cultural touch.

* **Dormitories:** On-campus international dormitories are safe, modern, and highly affordable compared to studio apartments in central Seoul. Facilities include high-speed internet, study rooms, communal kitchens, and fitness rooms.
* **Global Buddy Program:** The university matches incoming international students with local Korean students ("Duksung Buddies") to assist with campus adaptation, language exchange, and administrative tasks like alien registration and bank account creation.
* **Safety:** Dobong-gu is recognized as one of Seoul's safest and most residential districts, offering a quiet, secure environment for female students studying abroad.
