## School Overview
Founded in 1906, Dongguk University is a highly respected private university rooted in Buddhist values, located in the absolute heart of Seoul. The Dongguk University Korean Language Institute (KLI), established to promote Korean language education globally, serves thousands of international students annually. It is widely praised for its prime location adjacent to Namsan Mountain and the bustling shopping districts of Myeongdong and Dongdaemun.

## Academic Programs
### 1. Regular Program (10 Weeks per Semester)
The regular course runs for four semesters a year (Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter). It focuses on balanced communication, offering intensive training in speaking, listening, reading, and writing.
- **Schedule:** 5 days a week (Monday to Friday), 4 hours per day (total 200 hours per level).
- **Levels:** Level 1 (Beginner) to Level 6 (Advanced).
- **Tuition:** Approx. 1,650,000 KRW per semester (with a non-refundable application fee of 60,000 KRW).

### 2. Short-Term Intensive Course
A 3-week program designed for students wanting to experience the Korean language and cultural immersion during summer or winter vacations.

## Key Features and Advantages
- **Unmatched Location:** Situated at the foot of Namsan Mountain, the campus is beautiful and central. Line 3 (Dongguk University Station) connects you directly to all major hot spots in Seoul within minutes.
- **Pathways to Degree Programs:** Students who graduate from Level 4 or higher at Dongguk KLI receive preferential admission eligibility and significant tuition waivers/scholarships when entering Dongguk University's undergraduate or graduate schools.
- **Excellent Student Support:** The institute runs a robust 'Korean Buddy Program' pairing international students with domestic Dongguk students to facilitate rapid language acquisition and cultural integration.
- **State-of-the-Art Dormitories:** Located on or near the main campus, the Namsan Residence Hall provides modern housing facilities for language students with easy access to school amenities.

## Cultural Experience
Alongside structured classes, KLI arranges diverse extracurricular activities, such as traditional pottery making, K-Pop dance classes, temple stays, and tours of historical palaces in Seoul, helping students understand the depth of Korean culture.
