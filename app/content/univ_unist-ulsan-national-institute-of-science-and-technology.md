## University Overview

Established in 2007, the **Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST)** has rapidly risen to become one of South Korea's elite research-oriented universities, joining the ranks of KAIST, POSTECH, and GIST. Located in **Ulsan**, the industrial powerhouse of South Korea, UNIST is uniquely positioned to bridge world-class scientific research with real-world industrial applications.

UNIST stands out remarkably for its internationalized curriculum: **100% of all lectures are conducted in English**. This makes it an incredibly attractive and accessible hub for top-tier international students seeking world-class STEM education without a strict Korean language requirement.

---

## Academic Excellence & Research Focus

UNIST is highly specialized in science, engineering, and technology. Despite its young age, it regularly ranks among the top universities globally in citation impact, reflecting the high quality and relevance of its research.

### Key Research Pillars:
*   **Energy & Chemical Engineering:** Home to world-class research in secondary batteries (lithium-ion, solid-state) and solar cells. UNIST is globally recognized as a leading hub for green energy development.
*   **Materials Science:** Consistently ranked in the global top 50, pioneering research in nanomaterials, graphene, and advanced materials.
*   **Biomedical Engineering & Life Sciences:** Focusing on genomics, brain science, and advanced medical diagnostics.
*   **Information Technology & AI:** Strong programs in artificial intelligence, robotics, and big data, supported by the dedicated Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence.

## Comprehensive Financial Support & Scholarships

One of the most compelling reasons for international students to choose UNIST is its exceptionally generous financial aid package. 

### For Undergraduate Students:
*   **Tuition Waiver:** Admitted international students generally receive a **100% tuition waiver** for their program duration, contingent upon maintaining a minimum GPA.
*   **Living Stipend:** UNIST provides a monthly stipend (approximately 130,000 KRW to 350,000 KRW) to assist with basic living expenses and meal plans.
*   **Free Korean Language Programs:** Intensive Korean language classes are provided to help students integrate into society.

### For Graduate Students (MS / MS-PhD Integrated / PhD):
*   **Full Funding:** Tuition is completely covered, and graduate research assistants receive substantial monthly stipends (often exceeding 1,000,000 KRW for MS and 1,400,000 KRW for PhD) funded through university and national research projects.

## Admission Guide for International Students

UNIST operates two main intake periods: **Spring Semester** (March) and **Fall Semester** (September).

### Core Requirements:
1.  **Academic Records:** High school transcript and diploma (for Bachelor's) or university transcripts (for Graduate programs).
2.  **English Proficiency:** Since 100% of courses are in English, applicants must submit official scores such as **TOEFL iBT (minimum 80)**, **IELTS (minimum 6.0)**, or equivalent recognized English test certificates (unless they are native speakers).
3.  **Letters of Recommendation:** Typically, one or two recommendations from teachers/professors are required.
4.  **Personal Statement & Study Plan:** Detailing academic drive, previous research accomplishments (if any), and career objectives.

## Campus Life & Accommodation

UNIST boasts a modern, high-tech, and eco-friendly campus nestled alongside Gamak Lake. It operates as a residential campus, meaning almost all undergraduate and graduate students live in the on-campus dormitories.

### Housing & Amenities:
*   **Dormitories:** Modern double/single rooms equipped with high-speed internet, heating, air conditioning, and laundry facilities. The cost is highly subsidized and very affordable.
*   **Dining Services:** Multiple campus cafeterias serving both Korean and Halal/International dietary options.
*   **Sports Facilities:** A fully equipped sports center featuring a swimming pool, squash courts, gymnasium, and outdoor soccer/basketball courts.

## Industry Connections & Career Opportunities

Ulsan is the industrial capital of South Korea, hosting the global headquarters and massive manufacturing facilities of giants like **Hyundai Motor Group, Hyundai Heavy Industries, SK Innovation, and S-Oil**.

UNIST leverages this geographic advantage by maintaining close-knit R&D partnerships with these companies. Students have ample opportunities for internships, joint research projects, and corporate sponsorships. Graduates often secure lucrative positions in major South Korean conglomerates, public research institutions, or transition to prestigious PhD programs worldwide.
