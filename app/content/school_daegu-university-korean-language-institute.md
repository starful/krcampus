## School Overview
Daegu University Korean Language Institute (KLI) is a premier language center located on the expansive and picturesque Gyeongsan campus, just outside Daegu metropolitan city. Established to foster global communication and cultural exchange, the institute offers high-quality, systematic Korean language education to international students, diaspora Koreans, and foreigners residing in Korea.

## Curriculum & Academic Programs
The curriculum is structured systematically across six levels, from absolute beginner to highly advanced. Each semester runs for 10 weeks, providing 200 hours of intensive training in speaking, listening, reading, and writing. 
- **Integrated Approach:** Emphasis is placed on practical communication skills to help students adapt quickly to daily life in Korea.
- **TOPIK Preparation:** Specialized preparation classes are integrated to assist students in obtaining high scores on the Test of Proficiency in Korean (TOPIK), which is crucial for university admission.

## Housing & Facilities
Daegu University provides excellent on-campus accommodation through its student dormitories (Biho Dormitory / International House). These dormitories offer safe, clean, and highly affordable living options equipped with modern amenities, cafeteria access, gym facilities, and study rooms. Language students are highly prioritized for dormitory placement.

## Cultural Experiences & Support
Beyond the classroom, students participate in various cultural immersion activities, including:
- Traditional Korean arts, crafts, and food preparation.
- Excursions to historically significant neighboring cities like Gyeongju and Busan.
- A **Global Buddy Program** pairing international students with local Daegu University undergraduates to boost linguistic confidence and ease cultural adaptation.
- Academic counseling and support for students planning to transition into undergraduate or graduate programs at Daegu University.
