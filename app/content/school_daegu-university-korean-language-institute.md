# Guide: Daegu University Korean Language Institute

## 1. School Overview
Daegu University’s Korean Language Institute (KLI) provides high-quality language training for international students on its scenic green campus. With a total capacity of 500 students, the institute offers a supportive learning environment away from the high costs of the Seoul metropolitan area.

**Key Program Features:**
* **On-campus Dormitory Guaranteed:** Safe, affordable housing on campus is secured for all language students.
* **Generous Scholarship Benefits:** Financial awards based on academic excellence and high attendance.
* **TOPIK Preparation Classes:** Dedicated courses to prepare students for official proficiency exams.
* **Korean Buddy Program:** Pairs international students with domestic Korean students for linguistic and cultural exchange.
* **Cultural Field Trips:** Excursions to major historic sites like Gyeongju and Busan to experience Korean culture firsthand.

* **Address:** 201 Daegudae-ro, Jillyang-eup, Gyeongsan-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do, Republic of Korea

The KLI offers a systematic curriculum across Levels 1 to 6. The academic year is divided into four 10-week semesters. Each semester consists of 200 hours of study (20 hours per week, Monday through Friday, 4 hours per day).

## 4. Admissions & D-4 Visa Steps
International applicants studying for more than 90 days must apply for a D-4 (General Student) visa.

### Step-by-Step Process
1. **Application:** Submit the application form, passport copy, and academic certificates to KLI.
2. **Screening:** The university reviews documents and eligibility.
3. **Payment:** Approved applicants receive an invoice and pay tuition via bank wire.
4. **COA Issuance:** The university issues the Certificate of Admission (COA).
5. **Visa Application:** Submit the COA, financial proofs, and passport to the local Korean embassy.
6. **Entry to Korea:** Receive the visa, book flights, and move into the on-campus dormitory.

### Required Documents
* Completed application form
* Graduation certificate & academic transcript (Apostilled or Consular-verified)
* Bank balance certificate showing at least USD 10,000
* Passport copy (valid for at least 1 year)

## 5. TOPIK & University Pathway
The KLI prepares students for academic transition into undergraduate or graduate degrees.
* **TOPIK Training:** Intensive mock tests and writing prep classes are held before exams.
* **Admission Waiver:** Completion of Level 3 at KLI satisfies language requirements for undergraduate admission.
* **Pathways Scholarships:**
  * Language graduates entering Daegu University degree programs receive 30% to 50% tuition waivers based on TOPIK scores.
  * High academic scorers at the KLI are prioritized for internal university scholarships.

---

## 6. Dormitory & Living in the City
The main campus is in Gyeongsan, providing a lower cost of living than Seoul.

* **Guaranteed Housing:** All incoming language students are guaranteed on-campus housing. Dormitories feature double rooms with desks, beds, heating, and internet.
* **Dormitory Cost:** Approximately 600,000 to 950,000 KRW per semester (16 weeks), making it extremely economical.
* **Cost of Living:** Gyeongsan is very student-friendly, with food and transport costing up to 40% less than in Seoul.
* **Accessibility:** Regular buses connect the campus to Daegu Metro Line 1 & 2, taking 45-60 minutes to reach downtown Daegu.

## 7. FAQ

### Can I work part-time on a D-4 visa?
Yes, after 6 months of study, provided your attendance is above 90%. You must obtain a Part-time Work Permit from the immigration office.

### Is the on-campus dormitory guaranteed for language students?
Yes. Daegu University guarantees dormitory placement for all new international language institute students.

### What are the scholarship criteria for language students?
KLI offers 10% to 20% tuition discounts for the subsequent term to students with perfect attendance and top-ranking GPAs.

### Can I transfer to a Daegu University degree program?
Yes. Completing KLI Level 3 or passing TOPIK Level 3 allows you to apply for direct undergraduate admission.

### What happens if my D-4 visa is rejected?
If rejected, submit the official visa denial letter. The university will refund the paid tuition, excluding the 50,000 KRW application fee.
